ESG goals, met by the next generation用下一代,實踐企業的 ESG 目標

LCOY Taiwan connects companies' sustainability commitments with youth talent, green-job pipelines and a genuine UN climate platform — turning ESG reporting into real engagement.LCOY Taiwan 把企業的永續承諾,與青年人才、綠色職涯管道、以及真正的聯合國氣候平台串接起來——讓 ESG 從報告書變成真實的參與。

🌱 Green Jobs 🌐 UN Climate Stage 🤝 USR × CSR

💼 Youth green jobs青年綠色職業職缺(Green Jobs)

Taiwan's companies need to pay more attention to sustainability and ESG-related fields. As we expand opportunities, we should also identify whether the real issue is "a shortage of talent" or "a shortage of openings" — so that academia and industry develop talent that aligns with real market needs.

台灣企業在永續與 ESG 相關領域需更多重視。在擴大相關工作機會的同時,也要釐清目前的狀況到底是「人才不足」還是「職缺不足」;讓產學界培育的人才方向,能更貼近實際市場需求。

🌐 ESG & international climate action (COY / COP)ESG 與國際氣候行動(COY / COP)

Helping corporate sustainability (ESG) stakeholders build a foundational understanding of international climate conferences such as COY and COP. LCOY Taiwan enables Taiwanese companies and young people to connect with the global scope, and helps Taiwan engage in international climate governance and action frameworks.

協助 ESG 企業建立對國際氣候會議(如 COY、COP)的基本理解。LCOY Taiwan 讓台灣企業與青年能夠接軌全球氣候,協助台灣參與國際與聯合國的氣候治理與行動框架。

Why companies partner企業為何合作

Three ways an ESG partnership pays off企業合作的三大效益

01

A green-talent pipeline綠色人才管道

Internship matching and industry-academia links connect your teams with university students already trained in SDGs, climate action and project-based work.透過實習媒合與產學合作,串連已具備 SDGs、氣候行動與專題實作經驗的大學生人才。

02

Credible ESG engagement可信的 ESG 參與

Support for a UNFCCC-recognized youth platform is concrete, reportable ESG action — backed by UNFCCC and UNESCO-associated recognition, not just a slogan.支持一個 UNFCCC 認可的青年平台,是具體、可寫入報告的 ESG 行動——有 UNFCCC、UNESCO 相關認可背書,而非口號。

03

International visibility國際能見度

Youth projects and the LCOY Taiwan forum draw mainstream media coverage, raising your brand's sustainability profile at home and on the UN stage.青年專題與 LCOY Taiwan 論壇持續獲主流媒體報導,在國內與聯合國舞台同步提升企業的永續品牌形象。

Why it's credible為什麼值得信任

International recognition, not just a logo on a banner國際級背書,不只是看板上的一個 logo

LCOY Taiwan is hosted by Youth Network under UNFCCC YOUNGO recognition, with an exclusive partnership with PAC (People Achievement Consulting) — a recognized partner of NCDA (National Career Development Association) and a holder of GRI (Global Reporting Initiative) recognition, strengthening the project's professionalism and credibility in sustainability reporting and ESG standards.LCOY Taiwan 由 Youth Network 在 UNFCCC YOUNGO 認可下主辦,並與 PAC(People Achievement Consulting)簽署獨家合作。PAC 為 NCDA(國際生涯發展協會)認可夥伴,並持有 GRI(全球報告倡議組織)認可,強化本計畫在永續報告與 ESG 標準上的專業性與可信度。

Unit 1: Cell Phone

⬇ PDF

It is winter vacation and Wade is extremely bored. He takes out his cell phone and quickly dials his friend. There is no answer. He frantically calls another friend, but again there is no answer. Every one of his friends is on vacation and he is stuck at home alone. What will he do?

課文朗讀（真人）
現在是寒假，韋德非常地無聊。他拿出手機，並且很快地撥給他的朋友。沒有人回應。他發狂似地打電話給另一個朋友，但是還是一樣沒有人回應。他的每個朋友都在度假，只有他一個人坐困家中。他要做什麼呢?

生字 Key Words

extremely(adv.)極度地dial(v.)撥號frantically(adv.)狂熱地stuck(v.)困住alone(adv.)獨自地

進階學習 Go Further

signal(n.)訊號

My phone doesn’t get a signal in the elevator.

在電梯裡我的手機收不到訊號。

text(n.)簡訊

Send me a text when you have time.

當你有空時傳簡訊給我。

message(n.)留言

I left Tom a message.

我留了一通留言給湯姆。

recharge(v.)再充電

Your cell needs to be recharged.

你的手機需要充電。

on the phone(ph.)電話中

Maggie is constantly on the phone.

瑪姬老是在講電話。

小測驗 Quick Check

1. 「極度地」是哪一個英文字？

2. 「訊號」是哪一個英文字？

3. 「電話中」是哪一個英文字？

Unit 2: Playing Cards

⬇ PDF

Calvin and his friends are going to play a card game. First he shuffles the deck of cards. Then he quickly deals the cards to his friends and sets the deck in front of himself. Calvin picks up his cards and looks at his hand. He has some good cards and thinks that he will win the game.

課文朗讀（真人）
喀爾文和他的朋友們正準備玩撲克牌。他先洗牌，然後很快地發牌給他的朋友，並接著把紙牌放在他自己的前面。喀爾文拿起他的牌並且看著手中的牌。他有一些好牌，並且認為他將會贏得這場牌局。

生字 Key Words

shuffle(v.)洗deal(v.)發牌hand(n.)手中的牌

進階學習 Go Further

suit(n.)紙牌的花色

The four suits are clubs, diamonds, hearts and spades.

紙牌的四種花色是梅花、方塊、紅心及黑桃。

cheat(v.)行騙；作弊

Bob tries to cheat when he plays cards.

當鮑勃在玩牌時，他試圖想要作弊。

ace(n.)么點；A紙牌(亦稱愛司)

The ace of spades is the highest card in most games.

在大部分的牌局中，黑桃A是最大的牌。

a pair of(ph.)一對

Johnny has a pair of kings, three jacks and one queen.

強尼有一對國王、三張杰克以及一張皇后。

discard(v.)打出（無用的牌）

Paul draws a 4 of clubs and quickly discards it.

保羅抽到了一張梅花四，然後很快地把它打出去。

2. 「行騙；作弊」是哪一個英文字？

3. 「打出（無用的牌）」是哪一個英文字？

Unit 3: Lifting Weights

⬇ PDF

Colin and Elmer were very weak. They wanted to be stronger, so they decided to exercise more. Since then they have lifted weights almost every day. At first, Elmer could barely lift a light dumbbell off the ground, but now he can easily lift it over his head. Colin wasn’t very strong either, but his muscles are getting bigger each time he exercises.

課文朗讀（真人）
柯林和埃爾默以前身體很虛弱。他們想要變得更強壯，所以他們決定要多運動。從那時起，他們幾乎每天都在練舉重。剛開始，埃爾默只能勉強地將啞鈴舉起離地面而已，但是現在他可以輕易地將它舉過頭。柯林以前也不是很強壯，但是，每次的運動使他的肌肉變得愈來愈強壯。

生字 Key Words

lift(v.)舉起weight(n.)重量barely(adv.)僅；勉強地dumbbell(n.)啞鈴muscle(n.)肌肉

進階學習 Go Further

warm up(ph.)暖身

They warm up with light weights.

他們用輕一點的東西(輕量級的物品；東西)來暖身。

train(v.)訓練

The boys train almost every day to keep up their strength.

男孩們幾乎每天接受訓練以保持他們的體力。

barbell(n.)槓鈴

A barbell is longer than a dumbbell.

槓鈴比啞鈴還要長。

bench press(ph.)臥舉

Elmer can bench press 30kg.

埃爾默可以臥舉三十公斤。

curl(v.)彎舉

Colin can only curl 2kg.

柯林只能彎舉兩公斤。

2. 「暖身」是哪一個英文字？

3. 「彎舉」是哪一個英文字？

現在是給CP洗澡的時間。牠很喜歡洗澡，而且總是非常安靜的站著。梅在她公寓的陽台上幫牠洗澡。她用的是一條接在外面水龍頭的澆花水管。她捲起褲管，才不會弄濕。

CP總是抖動身體使身體變乾。

coat(n.)皮毛

A weekly bath keeps CP’s coat nice and shiny.

每週洗澡，讓CP的毛髮既好看又有光澤。

shed(v.)落下

A bath also removes hair that he is shedding.

洗澡也可以去除他正在掉落的毛髮。

Unit 5: Practicing the Guitar

⬇ PDF

Jenny is practicing for an upcoming concert. She reads the notes in her music book as she strums the guitar. Her hands are small, but she is still able to wrap them around the neck of the guitar. Her tiny fingers push on the strings to form a chord. The body of the guitar rests on her lap while she plays.

課文朗讀（真人）
珍妮正為了即將到來的演奏會練習。當她彈奏著吉他時，她看著音樂本上的音符)。她的手很小，但是她依然能夠握住吉他的琴頸。她纖細的手指頭按壓琴弦而形成一個和弦。當她彈奏時，琴身就倚靠在她的膝蓋上。

生字 Key Words

upcoming(adj.)即將來臨的note(n.)音符strum(v.)彈奏string(n.)弦chord(n.)和弦

進階學習 Go Further

tune(v.)（為樂器）調音

She always tunes the guitar before she plays.

她總是在彈奏吉他之前為它調音。

acoustic guitar(ph.)木吉他

Acoustic guitars are great for beginners.

木吉他對初學者是很好的。

pick(n.)撥片

Running the pick across several strings is called strumming.

用撥片橫彈過幾根弦叫作刷奏。

pluck(v.)彈；撥

Plucking one string at a time is called picking.

每次彈撥一根就稱之為撥弦。

fret(n.)弦馬；品

A standard guitar can have from 19-24 frets.

一把標準的吉他有19-24個音品。

Unit 6: Homeless Puppy

⬇ PDF

Susan has found a poor hungry puppy near a teashop. It is very thin and quite nervous of people. He probably hasn’t eaten in a long time. Luckily, Susan has some buns in her bag. She generously shares one with the starving little dog. He quickly gulps it down and then runs away.

課文朗讀（真人）
蘇珊在一間茶飲店附近，發現一隻既可憐又飢餓的小狗，牠非常的瘦弱，而且對人感到相當的緊張不安，牠大概已經有很長的一段時間沒有吃東西了。幸好蘇珊的包包裡有一些小圓麵包，她慷慨地分了一個給這隻挨餓的小狗。牠很快地把它吃掉，然後就跑走了。

生字 Key Words

luckily(adv.)幸運地generously(adv.)慷慨地starving(adj.)挨餓的gulp(v.)狼吞虎嚥地吃

進階學習 Go Further

stray(adj.)走失的

There are a lot of stray dogs living in the streets.

有很多的流浪狗在街頭生活。

flea(n.)跳蚤

Street dogs are often unhealthy and covered with fleas.

流浪狗通常不健康，而且身上佈滿跳蚤。

lost(adj.)迷路的

Some of the dogs are lost and can’t find their way home.

有些狗是迷路，找不到回家的路。

crouch(v.)蹲下

Susan crouches down to feed the nervous puppy.

蘇珊蹲下來餵這隻緊張不安的小狗。

sniff(v.)嗅、聞

It sniffs at the bun to make sure it is ok to eat.

牠聞一聞這小圓麵包，以確認它能吃。

Unit 7: Get Well Soon

⬇ PDF

Jason was very ill with the flu. He was so sick that his doctor admitted him into the hospital. The nurses came in to check on him every hour or so. They took his temperature and checked his pulse. They even hooked him up to an IV. After a few days, he was feeling better and went home.

課文朗讀（真人）
傑森得了流行性感冒病得很嚴重。因為他病情太重，所以他的醫生安排他住進醫院。護士大約每小時就進來確認他的狀況。她們為他量體溫，並檢查他的脈搏，甚至為他打點滴。幾天之後，他感覺比較好了，然後就回家去了。

生字 Key Words

admit(v.)接收入院temperature(n.)體溫pulse(n.)脈搏hook up(ph.)接上IV(n.)靜脈注射

進階學習 Go Further

hard(adj.)硬的

The hospital bed was small and hard.

醫院的病床又小又硬。

patient(n.)病人

He shared his room with another young patient.

他和另一個年輕的病患共用一間病房。

emergency room(ph.)急診室

If you are really sick or injured, you can go to the emergency room.

如果你病得很重或受傷，你可以去急診室就醫。

pill(n.)藥丸

He had to take pills three times a day before meals.

他必須在三餐飯前吃藥。

medicine(n.)藥

這服藥讓他康復得更快。

Unit 8: Board Games

⬇ PDF

Josh and his friends like to play board games. He thinks they are much more fun than video games. His favorite game is Monopoly. The idea of the game is to buy property and make money. You make money when another player lands on a space that you own. The winner is whoever makes the most money.

課文朗讀（真人）
喬許和他的朋友喜歡玩棋盤遊戲，他覺得棋盤遊戲比電動玩具有趣多了。他最喜歡的遊戲是大富翁。這個遊戲的概念是買房地產和賺錢。當別的玩家停在你擁有的土地時，你可以賺錢。賺最多錢的就是贏家。

生字 Key Words

Monopoly(n.)大富翁property(n.)房地產land(v.)落到whoever(pron.)無論誰

進階學習 Go Further

die(n.)骰子

Roll the die to see how many spaces to move.

擲骰子來決定移動幾步。

square(n.)小方格

You draw one of the cards in the middle if you land on a special square.

當你跳到一個特殊的小方格，你就在抽出中間的一張卡。

rulebook(n.)規則手冊

The rulebook tells you how to play.

規則手冊告知你遊戲怎麼玩。

game piece(ph.)棋子

Each player gets their own colored game piece.

每個玩家都能得到自己的彩色棋子。

doubles(n.)同點數

You have to roll doubles to get out of jail.

你必須擲出同點數才能出獄。

Unit 9: Paper, Scissors, Stone!

⬇ PDF

These cute little girls have a problem. They can’t figure out whose turn it is to sweep the driveway. They have decided to use an old game to solve their problem. It’s called Paper, Scissors, Stone (or rock). Jill wins the first round of the best two out of three. Pamela wins the second. The final and deciding round goes to Pamela. Her stone beats Jill’s scissors.

課文朗讀（真人）
這兩個可愛小女孩有一個問題。他們想不出是輪到誰要掃車道。她們決定用一個古老的遊戲來解決她們的問題。這遊戲叫做剪刀、石頭、布。三戰兩勝中吉兒贏得第一次。帕梅拉贏得第二次。最後決定性的那一次是帕梅拉獲勝。她的石頭打敗吉兒的布。

生字 Key Words

sweep(v.)打掃driveway(n.)車道solve(v.)解決scissors(n.)剪刀deciding(adj.)決定性的round(n.)一局

進階學習 Go Further

cover(v.)覆蓋

Paper covers stone.

紙包石頭(布贏石頭)。

cut(v.)剪；切

Scissors cut paper.

剪刀剪紙(剪刀贏布)。

smash(v.)搗毀

Stone smashes the scissors.

石頭搗毀剪刀(石頭贏剪刀)。

pump(v.)來回地動

They pump their fists five times while chanting “Paper, Scissors, Stone!”

他們喊剪刀、石頭、布時，拳頭來回擺動五次!

tie(n.)與…打成平局

If both show the same thing, it’s a tie and they do it again.

如果雙方出相同的東西，那就是平手，要再比一次。

Unit 10: Cleaning Your Ears

⬇ PDF

Calvin is cleaning his ears with a cotton swab. He carefully inserts the swab into his ear and swipes the sides of the ear canal. He pulls it out and it is covered in stinky earwax. Then he turns the swab over and uses the clean end to do it one more time. After that he can get another swab to do the other ear.

課文朗讀（真人）
卡爾文正使用棉花棒清他的耳朵。他小心翼翼地把棉花棒伸到他的耳朵，然後抹擦耳道的兩側。他把棉花棒抽出來，上面充滿了臭耳垢。接著他把棉花棒調過來，使用乾淨的那一端再清一次。之後，他可以再拿另一支棉花棒清另一側的耳朵。

wound(n.)傷口

你也可以使用棉花棒清潔傷口。

naturally(adv.)自然地

你的身體會自然地排出耳垢。

prevent(v.)預防

耳垢是粘性的，可以預防塵埃進入耳朵太深的地方。

Unit 13: Final Boarding Call

⬇ PDF

Connie looks at the bus schedule and sees that it is almost time to board. She quickly walks to the ticket booth and buys one ticket for Pitou. The bus is already outside the bus station at the boarding area. Connie hurries to the blue gates to board the bus. She is just in time. The final boarding call has just been announced.

課文朗讀（真人）
康妮看著公車時刻表，然後發現差不多該上車了。她快速地走到售票亭，並且買一張到埤頭的票。公車已經在車站外的候車區了，康妮趕緊到藍色登車門去搭公車。她及時趕到。最後的登車廣播剛播報。

她的最終目的地是埤頭。

The departure time will be in 30 minutes.

在30分鐘後將會出發。

fare(n.)車資

Mom, I need money for bus fare.

媽，我需要坐公車的錢。

stow(v.)收藏

Carry-on bags should be stowed under your seat.

隨身攜帶的行李應放在座位下。

Heavy luggage should be given to the bus driver to load.

厚重的行李應該交由公車司機來裝載。

Unit 14: The Carwash

⬇ PDF

Delores works at a carwash. Every day customers drop their cars off to be washed. First, Delores cleans the interior of the car. She vacuums the seats and floor then wipes down the dash. Next she washes the exterior with a pressure washer. The last thing to be done is to wash the windows.

課文朗讀（真人）
多洛麗絲在洗車場工作。每一天顧客將他們的車開來送洗。首先，多洛麗絲會清理車子的內部。她用真空吸塵器幫座位和地板吸塵，然後再擦拭儀表板。接著她會用高壓清洗機來清洗外部，最後要做的是清洗窗戶。

生字 Key Words

carwash(n.)洗車場customer(n.)顧客interior(n.)內部vacuum(v.)用真空吸塵器清掃dash(n.)儀表板exterior(n.)外部pressure(n.)壓力

進階學習 Go Further

The floor mats need to be shaken off.

這個腳踏墊需要抖一抖。

instrument panel(ph.)儀表板

The instrument panel has to be cleaned as well.

wax(v.)給…打蠟

Waxing and polishing is extra.

打蠟跟磨光都是附加的。

undercarriage(n.)(汽車的)底盤

She rinses off the undercarriage.

windshield wiper(ph.)擋風玻璃雨刷

She lifts up the windshield wipers to clean the windshield.

她立起雨刷來清洗擋風玻璃。

Unit 15: Playing Pool

⬇ PDF

Larry loves spending his free time shooting pool. In fact, if he is not in his room studying or at school, chances are that he is at the neighborhood pool hall. He is not the best pool player in the world, but he can beat most of his friends.

課文朗讀（真人）
賴里喜歡在他的空閒時間裡打撞球。其實上，如果他不是待在他的房間裡讀書或是待在學校，那他很可能就是在他們社區的撞球房。他並不是世界上最厲害的撞球選手，不過，他倒是可以打敗他大部分的朋友。

生字 Key Words

shooting pool(ph.)打撞球chances are(ph.)可能neighborhood(n.)鄰近社區pool hall(ph.)撞球房

進階學習 Go Further

cue ball(ph.)撞球臺上的白色球

The cue ball is the white ball that you hit.

母球就是撞球臺上的白色球。

pool cue(ph.)撞球桿

A pool cue is what you hit the cue ball with.

撞球桿就是你用來打母球用的球桿。

chalk(n.)巧克；白堊(塗在桿梢上防滑)

Chalk is rubbed on the end of the cue so that the cue doesn’t slip off the cue ball when you hit it.

巧克被用來塗在桿梢，這樣打母球時球桿才不會滑掉。

pool table(ph.)撞球桌

Pools tables are covered in green felt and have rubber rails along the edge.

撞球桌上鋪綠色的毛氈布，而且邊緣有橡膠圍欄。

pocket(n.)球袋

There are six pockets on a pool table.

一張撞球桌上有六個球袋。

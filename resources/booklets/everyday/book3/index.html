Unit 1: Get Your Shoes On, Keith!

⬇ PDF

Keith is getting ready to leave for school and has to finish getting dressed. He sits on a small stool to put on his socks. He lifts his leg and pulls the blue sock over his bare foot. Then he slips his shoes on. Now he is ready to go.

課文朗讀（真人）
基斯正準備上學去，他必須把衣服穿好。他坐在小板凳上穿他的襪子。他舉起腳，把藍色的襪子套進還沒穿好的那隻腳並往上拉，然後他很快地穿上他的鞋子。現在他已經準備好要出發了。

生字 Key Words

get dressed(ph.)穿衣stool(n.)凳子lift(v.)舉起bare(adj.)裸的slip(v.)匆忙的穿

進階學習 Go Further

tie one’s shoes(ph.)繫…的鞋帶

Don’t forget to tie your shoes.

不要忘了繫你的鞋帶。

uniform(n.)制服

Keith’s school uniform is blue and yellow.

基斯的學校制服是藍、黃相間的。

track suit(ph.)田徑服；運動服

We have to wear a track suit for gym.

上體育課時，我們必須要穿運動服。

sports uniform(ph.)運動服

Our sports uniforms have a tiger logo on the front.

我們的運動服前面有一隻老虎的標識。

shoelace(n.)鞋帶

Your shoelaces are untied.

你的鞋帶沒綁好。

Unit 15: Watering the Flowers

⬇ PDF

One of Scott’s household chores is to water the flowers. He checks them every day to see if the soil is dry. If they need water, he fills his watering can from the outside tap. Then he takes it to the flower bed and sprinkles the water over the plants.

課文朗讀（真人）
史考特的家事之ㄧ就是澆花。他每天巡視花朵，看看土壤是不是乾掉了。假如它們需要水的話，他就用外面的水龍頭將灑水壺裝滿，然後拿到花圃幫植物澆水。

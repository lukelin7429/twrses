Unit 1: Brushing Your Teeth

⬇ PDF

INCLUDEPICTURE "http://jnso.com/mh/bookup/brushteeth3.jpg" \* MERGEFORMATINET Tom is brushing his teeth. He brushes his teeth every morning. He brushes his teeth every day to keep them clean and healthy.

課文朗讀（真人）
湯姆正在刷牙。他每天早上刷牙。他每天刷牙，以保持牙齒的清潔與健康。

生字 Key Words

brush(v.)刷keep(v.)保持healthy(adj.)健康的

進階學習 Go Further

toothbrush(n.)牙刷

There are several toothbrushes in the cup on the shelf.

架上的杯子裡有好幾把牙刷。

toothpaste(n.)牙膏

Mary is squeezing some toothpaste onto her toothbrush.

瑪麗正將一些牙膏擠到她的牙刷上。

cavity(n.)蛀牙

Brushing your teeth can help to prevent cavities.

刷牙有助於預防蛀牙。

dentist(n.)牙醫

I hate going to the dentist.

我很討厭去看牙醫。

tap(n.)水龍頭

The toothpaste is on the sink next to the tap.

牙膏在洗手槽的水龍頭旁邊。

Unit 15: Flash Drive

⬇ PDF

INCLUDEPICTURE "http://jnso.com/mh/bookup/usingcomputer3.jpg" \* MERGEFORMATINET Sally has a Word file to save. She saves all her homework on her flash drive. This way she can bring it to school without printing it.

課文朗讀（真人）
莎莉要儲存一個Word檔案。她把全部的作業存到隨身碟。這樣她就可以把作業帶到學校而不用列印出來。

生字 Key Words

file(n.)檔案flash drive(ph.)隨身碟print(v.)列印

進階學習 Go Further

memory stick(ph.)記憶棒；隨身碟

A memory stick is like a small portable hard drive.

隨身碟就像是一個小型的可攜式硬碟。

port(n.)(電腦)接口

Plug the flash drive into a USB port.

請將隨身碟插入一個USB接口。

virus(n.)病毒

I need to scan my drives for viruses.

我需要掃描我的磁碟機看看是否有病毒。

email(n.)電子郵件

Jason sent me an email last week.

傑森上星期寄了一封電子郵件給我。

disc(n.)光碟片

Alice inserted a disc into the CD-ROM drive.

愛麗絲把一片光碟片放入光碟機中。

Unit 1: Parent Day

⬇ PDF

Yesterday was Parent Day at our school. Parents were invited to come and look in on our class. They got to see what goes on in the classroom and meet the teachers. They stood quietly in the doorway to watch.

課文朗讀（真人）
昨天是我們學校的家長參觀日，家長們受邀來看我們上課。他們可以看我們在教室的上課情形並且和老師們會面。他們安靜地站在門口觀看。

生字 Key Words

parent(n.)父或母invite(v.)邀請quietly(adv.)安靜地doorway(n.)門口

進階學習 Go Further

greet(v.)和…打招呼

My teacher greeted the parents at the door.

我的老師在門口和家長們打招呼。

observe(v.)觀察

Several parents observed our class.

有幾位家長看我們上課。

nervous(adj.)緊張的

My teacher was nervous because she was being watched.

我的老師很緊張，因為有人正盯著她看。

behavior(n.)行為

All of my classmates were on their best behavior.

我所有的同學都表現出最好的一面。

pay attention(ph.)注意

It is hard to pay attention when someone is watching you.

有人盯著你看時是很難專注的。

完整教學音檔

📖 課文教學（中文講解）

小測驗 Quick Check

1. 「父或母」是哪一個英文字？

2. 「和…打招呼」是哪一個英文字？

3. 「注意」是哪一個英文字？

進階學習 Go Further

溫蒂被自己的腳絆倒。

不要跳得太快，否則你可能會跌倒。

大衛會用跳繩耍特技。

Unit 6: Tire Pump

⬇ PDF

When I was about to leave for school this morning, I noticed that my bike had a flat tire. I searched the house and finally found a tire pump. I attached the line to the valve stem and filled the tire with air.

課文朗讀（真人）
當我今天早上即將上學時，我發現腳踏車的輪胎沒氣了。我在家裡找了一下，最後找到一個打氣筒。我把打氣筒的管子連接到輪胎的打氣孔上，並且幫輪胎充氣。

生字 Key Words

notice(v.)注意到flat tire(ph.)輪胎漏氣；爆胎search(v.)搜尋tire pump(ph.)打氣筒attach(v.)裝上；繫上valve stem(ph.)閥桿；打氣孔

進階學習 Go Further

base(n.)底座；基部

Stand on the base of the tire pump to keep it steady.

踩住打氣筒的底座使它保持平穩。

handle(n.)把手

Pump up and down on the handle.

用把手上下打氣。

air pressure(ph.)氣壓

Use a tire gauge to test the air pressure.

使用胎壓計測試胎壓。

spoke(n.)輪輻

The valve stem is between the spokes on the rim.

打氣孔在輪圏上的輪輻之間。

patch(v.)修補

The tire tube needs to be patched.

這個內胎需要修補。

完整教學音檔

Unit 8: Correct Your Mistakes

⬇ PDF

Students are always making mistakes when they write. Sometimes they misspell words or write sloppily. They could just cross the mistakes out, but it is better to do it again. This way their notebooks will be neater.

課文朗讀（真人）
學生寫字時總會出錯。有時候他們會拼錯字，或是寫得很潦草。他們可以把錯誤劃掉就好，但是最好是重新寫一次。這樣的話，他們的筆記本會比較整潔一點。

生字 Key Words

misspell(v.)拼錯sloppily(adv.)凌亂地；草率地cross out(ph.)劃掉；刪掉neat(adj.)整潔的

進階學習 Go Further

correction tape(ph.)修正帶

Cindy covers the misspelled word with correction tape.

辛蒂用修正帶蓋過拼錯的字。

不要用太多的立可白，否則沒辦法很快就乾。

messy(adj.)混亂的；凌亂的

湯姆的筆記非常凌亂。

tidy(adj.)整潔的

莎莉在書上做的筆記看起來很整齊，因為她寫字很工整。

smear(v.)塗抹使其變髒或模糊

Unit 14: Payphone

⬇ PDF

Tom is supposed to call his mother to come and pick him up. He finds a payphone near the library. He picks up the receiver and holds it up to his ear. Then he puts five dollars into the slot and dials his mother.

課文朗讀（真人）
湯姆該打電話請他媽媽來接他了。他在圖書館附近找到了公用電話。他拿起話筒，然後拿到他的耳朵旁。接著他把五塊錢投進投幣口，並且撥打電話給他的媽媽。

be supposed to(ph.)應該receiver(n.)電話聽筒slot(n.)投幣口dial(v.)撥

hang up(ph.)掛斷電話

Hang up the receiver to get your money back.

掛斷電話把你錢取回。

cord(n.)絕緣電線；軟線

The telephone cord is twisted.

電話線打結了。

wrong number(ph.)錯誤的號碼

I must have called the wrong number.

我一定是打錯電話了。

dial tone(ph.)撥號音

Wait for the dial tone before calling the number.

撥號前，要先等撥號音出現。

busy signal(ph.)忙線信號

I called Brian, but I got a busy signal.

我打電話給布萊恩，但是我聽到忙線的訊號。

Unit 15: Music Class

⬇ PDF

The first grade music class has been practicing for weeks. They are going to perform a song for their parents. Every day they have to practice their instruments together. It takes a lot of work to play in perfect time.

課文朗讀（真人）
這群一年級的學生在音樂課已經練習好幾個星期了。他們即將為他們的父母演奏一首歌曲。他們每天必須一起練習樂器。為了讓演奏整齊一致，必須下很大的功夫。

grade(n.)年級perform(v.)演奏instrument(n.)樂器in perfect time(ph.)整齊一致

Unit 1: Basketball

⬇ PDF

A lot of kids in Taiwan like to play basketball after school. In fact, basketball is Taiwan’s favorite pastime. There are many outdoor basketball courts everywhere. They are usually located near schools or parks.

課文朗讀（真人）
在台灣有很多小孩喜歡在放學後打籃球。事實上，籃球是台灣人最喜愛的娛樂活動。到處都有很多的戶外籃球場，它們通常位於學校或公園的附近。

生字 Key Words

pastime(n.)消遣；娛樂outdoor(adj.)戶外的court(n.)球場locate(v.)位於

進階學習 Go Further

pass(v.)傳遞

Kent passed the ball to Devin.

肯特傳球給戴維。

dribble(v.)運球

Dribbling is when you bounce the ball up and down.

運球是指你拍球讓它上下彈跳。

shot(n.)投籃

His shot hit the rim.

他的投籃打到籃框。

foul(n.)犯規

Dennis had three fouls in last night’s game.

在昨晚的比賽中，丹尼斯犯規三次。

backboard(n.)籃板

The ball bounced off the backboard and into the basket.

球碰到籃板後反彈進到籃框裡。

完整教學音檔

Unit 2: Gardening

⬇ PDF

Students are looking after the school’s garden. They are responsible for watering it, weeding it and picking the vegetables. It is hard work, but they enjoy doing it. Maybe some of them will grow up to be gardeners.

課文朗讀（真人）
學生正在照料學校的菜園，他們要負責澆水、除草，還有摘菜。這是件很辛苦的工作，但是他們樂在其中。也許他們當中有一些人將來長大後會成為園丁。

生字 Key Words

look after(ph.)照料responsible(adj.)需負責任的weed(v.)除草pick(v.)摘gardener(n.)園丁

進階學習 Go Further

plant(n.)植物

A weed is basically an unwanted plant.

雜草基本上是一種無用的植物。

root(n.)根

Make sure you pull out the roots, too, or it might grow back.

你務必要連根拔起來，否則它可能會再長出來。

grow(v.)種植

We grow all kinds of vegetables in our garden.

我們在我們的菜園裡種植各種蔬菜。

vine(n.)藤蔓

Peas and other vines need something to crawl up as they grow.

豌豆和其它的藤蔓植物生長時，需要有讓它們蔓延的東西。

harvest(v.)收成;收割

Most of the vegetables are harvested in the fall.

大部分的蔬菜都在秋天收成。

Unit 3: Band-aids

⬇ PDF

Kathy cut herself on a piece of broken glass. It wasn’t a bad cut, but she still didn’t want it to get infected. She cleaned around the cut first and then dried it. After that she covered it with a band-aid.

課文朗讀（真人）
凱西被一塊碎玻璃割傷。傷口雖然不深，但她還是不想讓傷口受到感染。她先把割傷的周圍清洗乾淨，然後把它擦乾。接著，她用一片OK繃把傷口包起來。

生字 Key Words

broken(adj.)破碎的infected(adj.)受感染的dry(v.)把cover(v.)覆蓋band-aid(n.)護創膠

進階學習 Go Further

stitch(n.)(縫合傷口的)縫線

Brent’s cut was deep and required stitches.

布蘭特的傷口很深，必須要縫幾針。

bleed(v.)流血；出血

Put pressure on the cut to stop the bleeding.

在割傷的部位加壓止血。

faint(v.)暈倒

My sister faints at the sight of blood.

我姊姊看到血會暈倒。

scrape(v.)擦傷；刮破

Jeremy fell off his bike and scraped his knee badly.

傑瑞米從他的腳踏車上跌落下來，他的膝蓋嚴重擦傷。

wound(n.)傷口

A band-aid will prevent germs from getting on the wound.

OK繃可預防細菌侵入傷口。

⬇ PDF

Kathy and her sisters are trying to blow up balloons. Her sisters are too small and can’t do it. Their lungs aren’t strong enough, but Kathy has no problem doing it. She puts the end of the balloon in her mouth and blows hard. But if the balloon gets too big, it will pop.

凱西幫她的妹妹們綁氣球。

那些氣球顏色很鮮豔。

氣球有許多的形狀，但圓形是最常見的。

Unit 13: A Musical Competition

⬇ PDF

I have been learning to play the recorder for two years. I have become very good at it, so my friends and I decided to enter a contest. I didn’t want to at first because I was very nervous, but my mom said that I had nothing to worry about.

課文朗讀（真人）
我學習吹直笛已經兩年了。我已經成為很棒的直笛手，所以我和我的朋友們決定要參加比賽。起初，我不想要參加，因為我很緊張。但是我媽媽說：沒有什麼好擔心的。

生字 Key Words

recorder(n.)直笛decide(v.)決定enter(v.)參加contest(n.)競賽nervous(adj.)緊張的worry about(ph.)擔心

進階學習 Go Further

stage fright(ph.)怯場

I got stage fright before the contest.

在比賽前，我會怯場。

competition(n.)比賽

This was my first recorder competition.

這是我第一次的直笛比賽。

note(n.)音符

I only played one note wrong during the song.

在這首歌曲中，我只吹錯一個音符。

judge(n.)裁判

The judges liked the song we played.

裁判們喜歡我們演奏的歌曲。

compete(v.)競爭；比賽

Hopefully we will compete together again.

但願我們能再度一起比賽。

Unit 14: Getting a Checkup

⬇ PDF

Derek is coughing and sneezing all the time. He has a cold. The doctor uses a stethoscope to check out his breathing. She holds the end of the stethoscope to his chest and listens. Now she can hear him breathe in and out to see if his lungs are okay.

課文朗讀（真人）
德理克不停的咳嗽和打噴嚏，他感冒了。醫生用聽診器檢查他的呼吸。她握著聽診器的末端貼著他的胸部，並且聆聽胸部的心音。現在，她可以藉由聽他的呼氣及吐氣，來查看他的肺部是否正常。

生字 Key Words

cough(v.)咳嗽sneeze(v.)打噴嚏stethoscope(n.)聽診器breathing(n.)呼吸chest(n.)胸膛

進階學習 Go Further

take one’s temperature(ph.)量...的體溫

The doctor had to take my temperature.

醫生必須量我的體溫。

deep breath(ph.)深呼吸

I took a deep breath so that the doctor could hear it.

我做了一次深呼吸，以便醫生能夠聽診。

sore throat(ph.)喉嚨痛

He has a sore throat and the chills.

他喉嚨痛及打冷顫。

fever(n.)發燒

I have a fever, but I feel cold!

我在發燒，但我卻覺得很冷!

take medicine(ph.)服藥

Derek will take medicine until he feels better.

德瑞克將會一直服藥，直到他覺得好點為止。

Unit 15: Open Wide

⬇ PDF

I hate going to the dentist for a checkup. He uses strange tools to poke at my teeth and gums. Sometimes it really hurts. My jaw gets sore, too, because I have to open my mouth so wide. The only good thing is that he gives me a prize if I sit still.

課文朗讀（真人）
我討厭到牙醫那裡檢查牙齒。他用奇怪的器具戳我的牙齒及齒齦。有時候這真的很痛。我的下巴也會很酸，因為我必須把我的嘴巴張得很大。唯一的好處是，如果我坐著不亂動，他會給我獎品。

生字 Key Words

dentist(n.)牙醫checkup(n.)檢查tool(n.)工具；器具poke(v.)戳gum(n.)齒齦；牙床prize(n.)獎品

進階學習 Go Further

filling(n.)填補物

I had to get a filling because I had a cavity.

我必須補牙，因為我有一顆蛀牙。

floss(v.)用牙線潔牙

The dentist said I need to floss every day.

牙醫說：我需要天天用牙線清理牙齒。

dental clinic(ph.)牙醫診所

The dental clinic smells like a medicine cabinet.

牙醫診所聞起來的味道像藥櫃一樣。

pull out(ph.)拔出來

The dentist had to pull out one of my front teeth.

牙醫必須拔掉我其中的一顆門牙。

rotten(adj.)腐爛的

One of my back teeth was rotten.

我後面的一顆牙齒蛀掉了。

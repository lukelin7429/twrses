Unit 1: Happy Birthday to Little Jimmy

⬇ PDF

Today is a very special day because it is little Jimmy’s first birthday. All of his family has come to celebrate with him. There is a beautifully decorated cake in the middle of the table. Jimmy looks hungrily at the cake as everyone claps and sings “Happy Birthday” to him.

課文朗讀（真人）
今天是一個非常特別的日子，因為今天是小吉米的一歲生日。所有的家人都與他一同慶祝。在桌子的中間有一個裝飾精美的蛋糕。當每個人都拍手為吉米唱生日快樂歌時，他飢餓似地盯著蛋糕看。

生字 Key Words

celebrate(v.)慶祝beautifully(adv.)美麗地decorated(adj.)裝飾的hungrily(adv.)飢餓地clap(v.)拍手

進階學習 Go Further

present(n.)禮物

Jimmy received many presents.

吉米收到很多的禮物。

paper plate(ph.)紙盤

They eat the cake on paper plates.

他們吃紙盤上的蛋糕。

excitedly(adv.)令人興奮地

Jimmy’s brother hides behind grandpa and giggles excitedly.

吉米的弟弟躲在爺爺的後面，並且很興奮地咯咯笑。

icing(n.)糖衣；(糕點的)糖霜

The white and yellow icing looks like a big egg on the cake.

黃白相間的糖衣看起來就像在蛋糕上面的一個大雞蛋。

lead(v.)帶領

Grandma happily leads the family in singing.

奶奶開心地帶領家人唱歌。

小測驗 Quick Check

1. 「慶祝」是哪一個英文字？

2. 「禮物」是哪一個英文字？

3. 「帶領」是哪一個英文字？

Unit 2: Wipeout

⬇ PDF

Marty always rides his bike carelessly. He daydreams and sings while he rides. One day he is going to have a serious accident. This time he luckily crashed into a wall. Next time it might be a car or truck. He needs to learn the rules of the road.

課文朗讀（真人）
馬帝騎腳踏車總是漫不經心。當他騎車時，他邊胡思亂想邊唱歌。總有一天他會發生嚴重的車禍。這次他很幸運的只是撞到牆壁。下一次，他也許就會撞到車子或是卡車。他需要學習道路交通規則。

Ben badly scraped his knee on the road.

班在路上嚴重地擦傷了膝蓋。

The basket on his bike is terribly bent.

他腳踏車上的籃子嚴重地彎曲。

in pain(ph.)在痛苦中

Carl lies on the ground in pain.

卡爾痛苦地躺在地上。

這並不是他第一次跌倒。

Unit 4: Water for Work

⬇ PDF

Penelope is filling her thermos with water before she goes to work. She pushes down on the blue handle. The water pours out of the bottle into her thermos. Then she puts the lid back on the thermos. Now she will have water all day long at work.

課文朗讀（真人）
潘娜洛普在上班前將她的保溫瓶裝滿水。她按下出水鈕上藍色的壓板，水就從桶子注入到她的保溫瓶裡。接著她將蓋子蓋回保溫瓶。現在，在她上班時，整天都有水可以喝了。

生字 Key Words

thermos(n.)保溫瓶push(v.)壓handle(n.)柄pour(v.)注；灌lid(n.)蓋子

進階學習 Go Further

attach(v.)附加

There is a small cup attached to the top of the thermos.

保溫瓶上面附了一個小小的杯子。

water filter(ph.)淨(濾)水器

The water filter cleans the water.

淨水器能使水質乾淨。

water cooler(ph.)飲水冷卻器

There is cold water in the water cooler.

飲水冷卻器裡有冰水。

tap(n.)水龍頭

The large bottle of water has a tap attached to it.

這大瓶水上面裝了一個出水龍頭。

regularly(adv.)定期地

She cleans her thermos regularly.

她定期地清洗她的保溫瓶。

Unit 5: The Photocopier

Luke needs to prepare for his English class. He carefully places a worksheet in the photocopier and closes the lid. Then he punches in the number of copies he wants. Once that is done, he pushes the start button.

你知道如何清除影印機卡紙嗎？

. Pull out the top tray and put more paper in.

把最上面的紙匣拉出來，然後放更多的紙張進去。

staple(v.)用釘書針裝訂

The copier can even staple papers together.

這台影印機甚至可以把紙張裝訂起來。

original(adj.)原始的

Put the original document face down.

將原稿的正面朝下放好。

Unit 6: Airing out the Blankets

⬇ PDF

Laura’s family doesn’t have a clothesline. Instead, they use chairs to air out their blankets in the driveway. They lay the blankets across the chairs so that they don’t touch the dirty ground. I hope they don’t hang their underwear outside, too!

課文朗讀（真人）
蘿拉的家沒有曬衣繩，他們用椅子代替，將被子放在車道上通風。他們將被子鋪在椅子上，如此一來，被子才不會碰到骯髒的地面。我希望他們不會也將內衣褲吊掛在外面。

生字 Key Words

clothesline(n.)曬衣繩instead(adv.)作為代替air out(v.)使通風driveway(n.)車道underwear(n.)內衣褲

進階學習 Go Further

germ(n.)細菌

Sunshine kills germs that live on blankets.

陽光會殺死住在被子裡的細菌。

fade(v.)褪色

The sun can fade colors and make things look old quickly.

陽光會使顏色褪色，而且很快地讓東西看來變得老舊。

breeze(n.)微風

A nice breeze helps to make sheets fresh again.

宜人的微風使床單又變得非常清新。

humid(adj.)潮溼的

Don’t hang out your laundry if it is too humid.

若天氣太潮溼的話，別把你的衣服掛在外面。

dark(adj.)黑暗的

Take the fresh blankets in before it gets dark.

在天黑之前，把潔淨的被子收進來。

Unit 13: The Arcade

⬇ PDF

There are lots of different games to play at the arcade, but Eugene likes the racing game the best. It is almost like driving a real car. It has a steering wheel, a gearshift, a gas pedal and a brake. Unlike a real car, this car runs on coins and not on gas.

課文朗讀（真人）
遊樂場有很多不同的遊戲可以玩，但是尤金最喜歡玩賽車遊戲。玩這個遊戲跟開真正的汽車幾乎一樣，它有方向盤、變速排檔、油門及煞車。和真正的汽車不一樣的是，這台車的運轉是用硬幣而不是汽油。

Each level gets more and more difficult.

每一關會變得愈來愈難。

Unit 14: Tire Race

⬇ PDF

Two students are getting ready to race. But this isn’t a normal race. They have to quickly roll a tire across the finish line. It sounds easy, but it is hard to roll the tire straight. And sometimes the tire rolls faster than you can run.

課文朗讀（真人）
有兩位學生正在準備比賽，但這不是一般的比賽。他們必須快速地滾動輪胎穿過終點線。聽起來很簡單，但是要讓輪胎筆直地滾動是很困難的。而且有時候，輪胎滾動的速度會比你跑得還要快。

Unit 15: The Produce Section

Tina and her friend have come to the supermarket to buy fresh vegetables. There are so many different vegetables in the cooler. Some are already packaged and some you put in a bag yourself. Tina checks out the mushrooms while her friend looks for a cabbage.

提娜和她的朋友到超市買新鮮的蔬菜。在冷藏櫃裡有很多不同的蔬菜。有些已經包裝好了，而有些則是要你自己拿來放在袋子裡。當提娜的朋友在找高麗菜時，她正看著蘑菇。

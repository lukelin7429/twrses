Unit 1: Preparing for School

⬇ PDF

Every morning, Cindy prepares for school. She gathers up all her homework and any books that she needs for the day. She then packs it all into her backpack. She also packs her pencil case, a snack and a jacket, in case she gets cold during the day. By the time she is finished packing, the backpack is quite heavy, but she has everything she needs for the day.

When packed and ready to go, she slings the backpack over her shoulder and heads for the door. She must get tired of carrying that heavy bag around all the time. At least she doesn’t have to worry about getting enough exercise.

課文朗讀（真人）
每天早上辛蒂準備上學，她收好所有的作業和當天需要的書，然後把它們裝進背包。她也把鉛筆盒、點心，以及一件外套放進去，以免白天的時候會冷。等她裝好之後，背包已經很重了，不過她已經有了當天所需要的每樣東西。 當她裝好書包準備上學時，她把背包掛在肩膀上，然後朝門的方向走去。對於老是得背那麼重的背包到處走，她一定感到很厭煩，不過，至少她不用擔心運動量不夠的問題。

閱讀理解 Questions

What do you carry in your school bag every day?

I usually carry a pencil case and any books I need for the day in my school bag.

What type of school bag do you prefer, a backpack or a regular book bag?

I prefer a backpack because it is safer to carry when I ride my bike.

Do you get tired of carrying your school bag around?

Yes, I’m tired of carrying my school bag around.

Unit 2: Jason Wipes His Nose

⬇ PDF

Jason has a runny nose. He reaches for a box of tissue on the table so that he can wipe it. His mother told him that he should use tissue and not the sleeve of his shirt because that is very disgusting. A single tissue is all he needs and he plucks one out of the box.

First he wipes his nose, but it still feels plugged so he covers his nose with the tissue and blows. It sounds like he is playing the trumpet. After doing this a couple of times, his nose is cleared and he can breathe much better.

Jason is a slob. 16 He leaves the germ-laden, 18 dirty tissue on the table. This is not the most hygienic of practices as someone else could catch his cold by touching it, but Jason is lazy and doesn’t care. His mother is always picking up after him. Will he ever learn?

課文朗讀（真人）
傑森流鼻涕了，他伸手去拿桌子上的面紙來擦鼻涕。他的媽媽以前就告訴他應該要用面紙擦鼻涕，而不是用襯衫的袖子，因為那樣是非常噁心的。他只需要一張面紙就夠了，他從盒子中抽出一張。 一開始，他先擦他的鼻子，但仍然感覺鼻塞，所以他用面紙掩住鼻子，並用力的吹氣，聲音聽起來很像在吹小喇叭。當他擤個兩三次之後，他的鼻子通了，而且呼吸也順暢多了。 傑森是個不愛乾淨的人，他把充滿細菌的面紙留在桌子上。這可不是個最衛生的習慣，因為其他的人若接觸到的話，可能會染上他的感冒。由於他太懶了，所以他並不在乎。他的媽媽總是幫他收拾善後。他以後會學乖嗎？

閱讀理解 Questions

Should you wipe your nose on your sleeve?

No, because it’s disgusting.

How does Jason clear his nose so that he can breathe better?

He blows his nose with a tissue.

What should you do with a dirty tissue?

You should throw it in the garbage can.

Unit 3: Wendell’s Big Bubble Trouble

⬇ PDF

Wendell’s grandmother always brings a treat for him when she comes to visit. This time she has brought him a pack of gum. Wendell thanks his grandma and then unwraps a piece of the bubblegum and stuffs it into his mouth. It is grape flavor, which is his favorite.

As he chews his big wad of gum, his little sister, Lily, asks if she can have some, too. Wendell is a very selfish boy and doesn’t like to share. He continues to chew his gum and blow bubbles as Lily looks on with envy. She is very sad. Eventually, she gives up and goes crying to her mother.

Meanwhile, Wendell keeps blowing bubbles as if nothing had ever happened. He couldn’t care less about anyone’s feelings other than his own. Then just as he is blowing the biggest bubble of his life, it pops in his face. The sticky gum flies everywhere. It’s on the floor, on his clothes, on his face and even in his hair. Perhaps after treating his sister so poorly, karma has snuck up behind him and burst his bubble.

課文朗讀（真人）
溫德爾的祖母來訪時，總會帶點零嘴點心給他。這回她帶給溫德爾的是一條口香糖。他向祖母謝謝後，隨即打開一塊泡泡糖，並且將它塞進嘴裡。這塊泡泡糖是葡萄口味的，這是他最愛的口味。 當他咀嚼著大塊的口香糖時，他的小妹莉莉問他可不可以給她一些。溫德爾是個非常自私的男孩，他不喜歡和別人分享。儘管莉莉在旁邊羨慕的看著，他仍繼續咀嚼他的口香糖，並且還吹著泡泡。她非常難過，最後她只好放棄，跑去向媽媽哭訴。 這時溫德爾若無其事地繼續吹著泡泡。他只在乎自己，完全不理會別人的感受。正當他吹著有生以來最大的泡泡時，泡泡砰的一聲在他臉上破掉了。黏黏的口香糖飛得到處都是，沾在地板、衣服、臉上，甚至沾在他的頭髮上。也許這是因為他對妹妹很不好，所以招致惡果偷偷溜到他的背後將泡泡戳破。(註：此處「惡果」採擬人化用法。)

What treat did Wendell’s grandmother bring for him?

She brought a pack of gum for him.

Does Wendell share his treat with Lily?

No, because he is a very selfish boy.

Where does the gum go when the big bubble pops?

It’s on the floor, on his clothes, on his face and even in his hair.

Unit 4: Who’s at the Door?

⬇ PDF

Buzz! The doorbell is ringing. Myles wonders who it is as he races for the intercom.

“Who is it?” he inquires.

“Pizza delivery,” a strange voice replies.

“I didn’t order any pizza,” Myles claims. “Are you sure you have the right address?”

“Is this number 63 Julin Street?” he asks.

“Uh, yeah.”

“Then I have ten pepperoni pizzas for you,” the voice insists.

“There must be a mistake! I didn’t order any pizza!” Myles argues.

The voice on the other end breaks into a roar of laughter.

“Gotcha!” Carl shouts.

“Carl, you idiot!” Myles says, breathing a sigh of relief. “You really had me going.”

“Yeah, I know,” Carl snorts. “Buzz me up so we can watch TV.”

Myles presses a button on the intercom and the front door unlocks. He then runs to hide so that he can pay back Carl by scaring him.

課文朗讀（真人）
鈴...！門鈴響起。麥爾斯跑去接對講機時，心裡在想到底會是誰呢？ 「請問是哪位？」他問。 「送披薩的，」一個陌生的聲音回答他。 麥爾斯說：「我沒有訂任何的披薩，你確定住址正確嗎？」 「這是竹林街63號嗎？」他問。 「嗯，是的！」 「那我有十個義大利辣味香腸披薩要給你，」這個人堅持地說。 「一定是出了差錯，我沒有訂任何的披薩啊！」麥爾斯辯稱。 對講機的另一頭傳出了一陣爆笑聲。 「上當了！」卡爾大叫。 「卡爾你這個白癡，」麥爾斯鬆了一口氣說：「我真的被你騙了。」 「是啊，我知道，」卡爾嘲諷地大笑。「幫我開門吧，我們一起看電視。」 麥爾斯按了對講機的按鈕，前門就打開了。接著他跑去躲起來，以便要嚇嚇卡爾來報復他。

No, he doesn’t know who is at the door.

Unit 5: Naptime at School

⬇ PDF

The teacher in this classroom must be extremely boring, as all the children have fallen asleep. Actually, it is part of the daily routine for most Taiwanese school children. From 12:00 till 12:30 they eat their lunch. This is followed by a nap that lasts until about 1:20. Some teachers believe that by having the children take a short nap after lunch they will be refreshed and ready to learn for the second half of the day.  　　The standard position seems to be hunched over with your head resting on folded arms. Some students say that this is a very uncomfortable position and claim that it gives them sore arms. Others say that being hunched over while having a full belly makes them feel a bit sick. Regardless of 22 whether or not they like it, it is the policy of some teachers. I hope those students who don’t like it get used to it, as there is no room in the classroom or the school budget for dozens of 28 beds.

課文朗讀（真人）
這間教室的老師上課一定極為無聊，因為所有的小朋友都睡著了。事實上，這是大多數台灣學校小朋友每天例行公事的一部份。十二點到十二點半他們吃午餐，之後的休息持續到大約一點二十分。一些老師相信，讓這些學生吃完午餐後稍微小睡一下，可以讓他們恢復精神，然後準備好學習下午的課程。 午睡的標準姿勢似乎是趴著，然後頭靠在交叉的手臂上面。有些學生說這是非常不舒服的姿勢，並宣稱這個姿勢會讓手臂痠痛。其他學生則說肚子飽飽的時候趴著睡，會讓他們覺得有一點不舒服。不管他們喜不喜歡，這是一些老師的規定。我希望那些不喜歡的學生能夠適應它，因為教室沒有空間，或者學校也沒有預算能夠買幾十張床放在教室。

閱讀理解 Questions

Why are all the children sleeping?

Because it’s a part of their daily routine.

What is the problem with sleeping hunched over?

It’s a very uncomfortable position.

Why can’t the school buy beds?

Because there’s no budget. Besides, there is no room in the classroom.

Unit 6: The School Library

⬇ PDF

Students borrow a lot of books from libraries. Whether you are researching a topic for your homework or just need a book to relax with for the weekend, you can just check out a book from your school’s library. You don’t even have to pay for it.

These students are browsing the shelves for a book, but there are so many to choose from that finding a book can be difficult. All the books are organized into categories or alphabetized by author or title. Still there are times when you just can’t find that specific book you’re looking for. In these instances, try using the library’s database to speed up your search. It could save you a lot of time by locating the book for you.

If you decide to take a book home for a while, you will need to check it out. Simply take the book to the librarian. They will scan the book and your library card into the computer. This will give them a record of who has the book and when it is due back. Make sure your book comes back on time. In public libraries you can be charged a fee for any overdue books. If it happens too often, they may revoke your library card.

課文朗讀（真人）
學生從圖書館借很多書，不管你是為了寫作業而研究一個主題，或者只是想利用週末看書來放鬆心情，你都可以到學校的圖書館借書，甚至不用花你任何錢呢！ 這些學生正在瀏覽書架找書，但是因為有太多的書可供選擇，所以要找到一本書可能很困難。所有的書都有分類編排，或依照作者的姓名或書名的字母順序排列。然而，有時候你還是會找不到某本你要的書。遇到這種情況，你可以試著使用圖書館的資料庫來加快查詢速度；它可以找出書的位置，為你節省很多時間。 如果你決定要把書帶回家看一陣子，你必須要辦理借閱手續。只要把書交給圖書館員，他們就會把這本書以及你借書證的資料掃描到電腦中。這樣一來，他們便會有這本書被誰借走以及何時到期的記錄。請務必準時歸還書籍，在公立圖書館，如果你逾期還書可能會被罰錢。如果這種情形經常發生的話，他們也許會取消你的借書證。

閱讀理解 Questions

What are some of the reasons students borrow books?

They borrow books to do their homework or to research things for papers.

Why is a database useful for finding a book?

It is useful because it can speed up your search.

How do you check a book out?

You check it out by taking it to the librarian.

Unit 7: Grocery Shopping

⬇ PDF

On her way home from school, Jenny decided she needed to stop at the local grocery store. As she entered, she looked around and found a shopping basket. She placed the basket on a cart to make a mini-shopping cart. It was easier for her to push the cart rather than carry the basket. The aisles were organized neatly and Jenny pushed the cart down each one to find what she needed. Each item she wanted she took off the shelf and placed in the shopping cart. Jenny, being a bargain hunter, preferred the items that were on sale. Her thriftiness allows her to save a lot of money whenever she shops.

After filling the basket, she lined up at the checkout. When it was her turn, she emptied the contents of the basket onto the counter. The cashier scanned each item. A couple items wouldn’t scan correctly so the cashier had to key in the bar code manually. When the cashier had finished scanning everything, she hit the total button. Jenny handed her a five hundred dollar bill. The cashier checked the bill to make sure it wasn’t counterfeit and then slipped it into the cash register. She then counted out the change and handed it back to Jenny. Jenny thanked her, took her bag of groceries and headed for home.

課文朗讀（真人）
在從學校返家的途中，珍妮決定必須去一趟當地的雜貨店。當她一進門時，四周看了一下，並找到了一個購物籃。她把它放在推車上，就變成了一台迷你購物車。對她來說，推購物車比提籃子還要輕鬆。走道的東西排放地非常整齊。珍妮推著購物車，一排一排地尋找她需要的東西。她把想買的每樣東西從架上拿下來，然後放入購物車中。由於珍妮是一個喜歡購買便宜貨的人，所以她偏好特價商品。由於她的節儉，讓她每次購物時都能節省很多的錢。 當籃子裝滿東西後，她便到收銀台排隊結帳。輪到她的時候，她把籃子的東西全部拿到櫃台上面。收銀員將每件商品一一掃描，但是其中有一兩樣無法正確地掃描，所以收銀員必須用手輸入條碼。當她把每樣東西都掃描過後，便按了合計的按鍵。珍妮拿了一張五百元的鈔票給她，收銀員便檢查這張鈔票，以確認是否為偽鈔，接著她就把它塞入收銀機。她算出要找的零錢，然後把它交給了珍妮。珍妮對她說聲謝謝，便提著一包雜貨回家。

閱讀理解 Questions

Where was Jenny going when she decided to stop and buy groceries?

She was going home.

Because she is a bargain hunter.

Unit 8: Dad’s New Car

⬇ PDF

My dad bought a brand-new car yesterday. It is the coolest car ever. It has everything you could want, like nice soft seats, air-conditioning, power windows, and even a DVD player in the back seat. Silver wouldn’t have been my first choice for a color, but then again I didn’t have to pay for it, so I guess it’s ok.

I like the new car so much that I am going to ask my parents if I can live in it. That would be so cool. It has everything I could possibly want—except for maybe a bathroom. I can see myself sitting in it during a hot summer day, watching a movie, sipping a drink and cooling off with the air-conditioning blasting away. Life couldn’t get much better than that.

My little sister really likes the new car, too, but not as much as me. Her favorite part is the sunroof. Dad says we aren’t allowed to stick out our heads while the car is moving or they’ll be chopped off, but we still like to do it when the car is parked.

I am actually looking forward to long trips now. I used to hate them, but then again our old car was a piece of junk. Whenever we went on a trip, the car would overheat and we weren’t allowed to use the air-con because that only made it heat up faster. We shouldn’t have to worry about that anymore;23 plus, we can watch movies. It’s like a dream come true.

課文朗讀（真人）
昨天我爸買了一台全新的車子。它是有史以來最酷的車子。任何你可能想要的配備它都有了，像是精巧又柔軟的座椅、空調、電動車窗，甚至後座還有DVD播放器。就顏色而言，銀色並不是我的第一選擇，但是話說回來，買車的錢並不是我出的，所以我想這是可以接受的。 我非常喜歡這台新車，喜歡到我想問我的父母是否可以住在這台車子裡面，那將會是很棒的。任何我可能想要的配備都在裡面了，也許除了廁所之外吧！我可以想像夏天的時候，我坐在裡面看電影、喝飲料，還吹著涼爽的冷氣。人生中沒有比這樣更美好的了！ 我的小妹也很喜歡這台車，但是沒有像我這麼喜歡。她最喜歡的部份是天窗。 爸爸說：當車子開動的時候，不准我們把頭伸出車外，不然頭會被切斷。但是，當車子停好時，我們仍然喜歡把頭伸出去。 我真的很期待去長途旅行。以前我很討厭長途旅行，話說回來，這是因為我們的舊車就像垃圾一樣爛，坐起來很不舒服。每次我們去旅行，車子都會過熱，而且我們不能使用空調，因為這會使溫度上升的更快。我們應該再也不必擔心這個問題了。此外，我們還可以看電影。這就像是美夢成真啊！

Unit 9: My Dog Rex

⬇ PDF

This is my dog Rex. He is small with short legs and floppy ears. His coat is light brown with a few black patches. I don’t know what breed he is, but he looks like a beagle crossed with a typical Taiwanese mutt.

He was just a puppy when Dad found him on the street and was in very bad condition. It looked like he hadn’t eaten for days or longer, and was covered with ticks. My father took pity on him and brought him home.

Mom immediately took Rex to the vet to have him checked out and to make sure that he was ok. The vet gave him a couple of shots, a flea bath and treated a couple of sores on his legs. It cost quite a bit to have him taken care of, but it didn’t matter because our family couldn’t stand to let an animal suffer like that.

Once at home, he was a bit nervous, but settled down shortly after. He could tell that he was in a safe place.

The next day, Dad went out and bought him a doghouse. It was small, white, and had a peaked roof 20 just like a real house. Rex seemed to like it too and went in to sniff 21 around right away.

Dad also bought a collar and a leash. Most of the family is gone during the daytime, so we needed something to tie him up in the yard with. It restricted him a bit, but I don’t think he minded. He was happy to have a good home.

My family has had Rex for almost seven years now and I can’t imagine life without him. He is so happy to see us whenever we get home and always greets us with a big sloppy kiss. He is also great at cheering us up when we are feeling down. He is the best dog ever, and I hope he lives a long, long time.

課文朗讀（真人）
這是我的狗－雷克斯。他是一隻腿很短而且耳朵下垂的小狗。他的毛是淺棕色的，並且有一些黑色的斑點。我不知道他的品種，但他看起來像是小獵犬和傳統台灣土狗的混種。   當我爸在街上發現他時，他還很小，而且狀況非常不好。那時他看起來似乎有好幾天沒吃東西了，或甚至更久一點，而且全身都是蝨子。我爸看他很可憐，於是將他帶回家。   媽媽立刻帶他去獸醫那兒檢查，以確認他的狀況無虞。獸醫給他打了幾針、幫他洗澡清除跳蚤，並且治療他腳上的幾處傷口。照顧他花了我們不少錢，但這不重要，因為我們家人無法忍受讓一隻動物受那種苦。   剛到我家時，他有點緊張，但很快心就安定下來了。他知道他待的是一個安全的地方。 隔天，老爸外出幫他買了個狗屋，這個小狗屋顏色是白的，屋頂尖尖的，看起來像是真的房子。雷克斯似乎也很喜歡他的窩，並且馬上走進去到處嗅嗅。   爸爸也買了一個項圈和鍊條。白天時，大部分的家人都不在，所以我們需要東西來把他拴在院子裡。這有點限制了他的活動範圍，但我認為他不會介意，他很高興能有一個好的住所。   雷克斯來我們家已經快七年了，我無法想像沒有他的生活。每當我們回家時，他都很高興看到我們，而且總是用溼溼的舌頭熱情地舔著我們以示歡迎。當我們心情沮喪時，他也很會逗我們開心。他是有史以來最棒的狗，我希望他能活很久很久。

Unit 10: Belinda Has a Flat

⬇ PDF

Belinda’s back tire seemed a little flat as she rode to school this morning, so she is checking the air pressure in it. As she squeezes it with her hands, it feels noticeably soft. If she were to ride on it any further, it may damage the tire or tube.

To fill the tire up, she uses a small air pump. First, she attaches the air hose to the valve stem located between the spokes and locks it in place.

Next, she begins to fill the tube with air by pumping up and down on the handle. It takes only a few minutes before the tire is hard again, but there is a problem. Belinda hears a hissing sound coming from somewhere on the tire. Examining the tire more closely reveals a small nail that has punctured it. Before she can ride again, she will need to buy a new tube or patch the old one. For now, she will have to be content with walking.

課文朗讀（真人）
今天早上，布蘭達騎腳踏車上學時，她的後輪似乎有點洩氣了，所以她現在正在檢查胎壓。當她用手擠壓後輪時，輪胎感覺明顯變軟了。如果她再騎下去，腳踏車的外胎或內胎可能會受損。 她使用一個小型的打氣筒幫輪胎打氣。首先，她將送氣的軟管接到輪輻間的閥桿，並且把它固定好。 接著，她開始用把手上下施力為輪胎打氣。只花了幾分鐘的時間，輪胎就變硬了。但是有一個問題，那就是布蘭達聽到輪胎的某處發出嘶嘶聲。在更加仔細地檢查輪胎之後，發現有一根小鐵釘刺穿了輪胎。她將必須換新的內胎，或是把舊胎修補一下，才可以繼續騎。現在，她就只能安步當車了。

Unit 1: Parent-Teacher Association

⬇ PDF

The Sihu Senior High School Parent Teacher Association recently elected a new president. An inauguration ceremony was held to officially swear in the new president. The Chinese name for this ceremony is 交接典禮, which literally translated means passing-it-on. In other words, the old president passes the torch to the new one.

Members of the PTA signed an attendance sheet at the door. This is a formality that takes place before all PTA gatherings so that there is documentation of who is present. The secretary of the group, who is most likely a volunteer, usually looks after these records.

Once signed in, members were then asked to pin red ribbons to their shirts or jackets. Mr.Wu had difficulty attaching the pin to his lapel, so he asked Mary for assistance. She opened the lapel, carefully stuck the pin through it and then put the clasp on.

When the new president was formally sworn in, a handshake from the former president made it official. He was then presented with flowers and gifts from the PTA to congratulate him. To finish everything off, he gave a short speech to thank everyone for their support.

課文朗讀
四湖高中家長教師會最近選出了一位新會長。學校舉行就職典禮，正式讓新會長宣誓上任。這個典禮的中文名稱是「交接典禮」，字面意思就是把事情傳下去；換句話說，就是舊會長把責任交棒給新會長。家長教師會成員在門口簽到。這是所有會議前都會進行的正式程序，目的是留下出席紀錄。團體的秘書，通常可能是志工，會負責保管這些紀錄。簽到後，成員被要求把紅絲帶別在襯衫或外套上。吳先生不太會把別針別在翻領上，所以請瑪麗幫忙。她拉開翻領，小心地把別針穿過去，再扣上扣環。新會長正式宣誓就職時，前任會長與他握手，使交接正式完成。接著家長教師會送上花和禮物祝賀他。最後，他發表簡短演說，感謝大家的支持。

Unit 2: Bending and Stretching

⬇ PDF

Look at the children stretching. They bend and stretch to make themselves more flexible. Stretching before any exercise is a good idea. It reduces the chances of injury.

In dance class, flexibility is very important, so stretching is a must. The more flexible you are, the more dance moves you can do. The more dance moves you can do, the better a dancer you are. But it is also important to stretching exercises properly, or you could hurt yourself.

First, their teacher shows them how to do each stretching exercise. Then the students try it. She watches to see that they do it right. If a student is doing something wrong, the teacher can help him or her out.

In the stretching exercise in the picture, the students lie on their stomachs and try to lean back as far as they can while bending their legs at the knees. Some of the girls are so flexible that they can touch the back of their heads with their toes. The teacher helps one boy to stretch way back. Hopefully she doesn’t break the boy in half!

課文朗讀
看看孩子們正在伸展。他們彎曲並伸展身體，讓自己變得更柔軟。任何運動前伸展都是好主意，因為可以降低受傷的機率。在舞蹈課中，柔軟度非常重要，所以伸展是必要的。你越柔軟，就能做越多舞步；能做越多舞步，就越能成為好的舞者。不過，正確做伸展也很重要，否則可能會受傷。首先，老師示範每一個伸展動作，然後學生試著做。老師會觀察他們是否做對；如果有學生做錯，老師就能幫忙。照片中的伸展動作是學生趴著，膝蓋彎曲，並盡可能往後仰。有些女孩柔軟到可以用腳趾碰到後腦勺。老師正在幫一個男孩往後伸展。希望她不要把那個男孩折成兩半！

Unit 3: Buying Tree Decorations

⬇ PDF

I get very excited every year when it is time to set up and decorate the Christmas tree. This year was even more special because Mom decided to throw out the old tree and buy a new one. We had been using that old tree for more than five years and it was starting to look a little worn-out.

My parents don't believe in using real trees. They think it is very wasteful to chop down a healthy tree that is just going to be used for a few days and then be thrown out. That is why we have an artificial one. If you ask me, they look just as good anyway.

The store we went to had lots of Christmas decorations. I swear that they had enough tinsel and garland to make Jay Chou jealous. There were hundreds of tree ornaments there, as well. Some of them were the pretty glass ones that I like, but Mom opted for the cheap plastic kind because my cat would probably just break anything that was too fragile anyway.

Most of the ornaments came in packs of six or eight. I wanted to get blue and yellow ones, but my mom wanted green and red. Because we couldn’t agree on the colors for the tree, we picked up 4 variety packs.

We didn't have to buy new lights this year, but we still looked at them anyway. I wished that we would buy new ones so that I wouldn’t have to untangle the old strands like I do every year. And ours don't blink either, like some of the new fancier ones that make the tree look like it is sparkling. Oh well, maybe next year.

課文朗讀
每年到了架設和裝飾聖誕樹的時候，我都非常興奮。今年更特別，因為媽媽決定丟掉舊樹，買一棵新的。我們那棵舊樹已經用了五年以上，開始看起來有點破舊。我的父母不贊成使用真正的樹。他們覺得砍下一棵健康的樹，只用幾天就丟掉，非常浪費。所以我們用的是人造聖誕樹。要我說，它們看起來也一樣好。那家店有很多聖誕裝飾。我敢說那裡的金銀絲和彩帶多到會讓周杰倫嫉妒。店裡也有上百種聖誕樹裝飾品。有些是我喜歡的漂亮玻璃裝飾，但媽媽選了便宜的塑膠款，因為我的貓大概會把太易碎的東西打破。大多數裝飾品是六個或八個一包。我想買藍色和黃色的，但媽媽想買綠色和紅色。因為我們無法決定聖誕樹的顏色，所以買了四包綜合包。今年我們不必買新的燈串，但還是看了一下。我希望我們會買新的，這樣我就不用像每年一樣解開舊燈串。舊燈串也不會閃爍，不像一些新的漂亮燈串，會讓樹看起來閃閃發光。算了，也許明年吧。

Unit 4: Opening Ceremony

⬇ PDF

Yesterday there was a track meet at my school. I didn't take part in any of the events or the opening ceremonies, but I still enjoyed watching everything. Even though I am not really into track and field, at least I didn’t have to go to class for the day.

The opening ceremony seemed quite official. It was almost like watching a real Olympic ceremony. A flag bearer led each class’s athletes onto the field. The procession marched once around the track, smiling and waving to the crowd.

After one loop around the track, each class lined up behind their respective leaders and flag bearers. It was all done in a very militant style. I was surprised to see that even some of my unruly classmates were capable of standing so still and being so quiet for more than ten seconds.

Once everyone was standing in place, six cheerleaders ushered out the school flag. They marched once around the track in their knee-high boots and short skirts. They must have done their job because the whole crowd cheered as they passed.

All in all, it was pretty impressive that a bunch of my schoolmates could put together such a wonderful opening ceremony. Maybe they are more organized than they seem. They certainly did a better job than I would have believed.

課文朗讀
昨天我的學校舉行田徑運動會。我沒有參加任何比賽或開幕典禮，但我仍然很享受觀看一切。雖然我不是很喜歡田徑，至少那天不用上課。開幕典禮看起來相當正式，幾乎像在看真正的奧運典禮。每班的掌旗手帶領運動員進入場地。隊伍繞著跑道走一圈，微笑並向觀眾揮手。繞完一圈後，每個班級都排在各自的領隊和掌旗手後方。整個過程非常有軍事化風格。我很驚訝看到連一些平常難管教的同學，也能站得這麼直、安靜超過十秒。大家站定後，六位啦啦隊員引領校旗出場。她們穿著及膝長靴和短裙，繞跑道走了一圈。她們一定做得很好，因為經過時全場都在歡呼。總的來說，一群我的同學能合力完成如此精彩的開幕典禮，真的令人印象深刻。也許他們比看起來更有組織。他們的表現確實比我原本相信的還要好。

Unit 5: Pumps in the Park

⬇ PDF

Jack and Jill are playing with the pumps in the park. Not so long ago, everyone had to use pumps to get water out of the ground. This was before electric pumps.

I can remember filling the water trough for Grandma's cows by hand. It took a long time to fill the very large trough. It had to be done two times a day because the cows drank a lot. If it was really hot out, then it had to be done even more often because the cows would drink even more water. It was hard work, but it made me a strong boy.

When my mother was a girl, her and her family had to pump their own water for everything. If you wanted to take a drink you had to pump. If you wanted to wash your clothes, you had to pump. If you wanted to take a bath, you had to pump. Not to mention all the water they had to pump and haul by hand for the farm animals. Pump, pump, pump, all day long. Imagine having to pump water every time you turn on the tap!

Jack and Jill find it fun to pull on the long handle and to watch the water come out of the spout. It splashes on the ground as they giggle and dance about. They are having the time of their lives, but I am sure that it would not be so much fun if they had to do it every day like my mother’s family did.

課文朗讀
傑克和吉兒正在公園裡玩抽水幫浦。不久以前，每個人都必須用幫浦把地下水抽出來，那是在電動抽水機出現以前。我還記得曾經用手幫奶奶的牛把水槽裝滿。那個很大的水槽要裝滿需要很久。因為牛喝很多水，所以一天必須裝兩次。如果天氣很熱，還得更常裝，因為牛會喝更多水。那是辛苦的工作，但也讓我成為強壯的男孩。當我媽媽還是女孩時，她和家人所有用水都得自己抽。想喝水，要抽水；想洗衣服，要抽水；想洗澡，也要抽水。更不用說他們還得為農場動物抽水，並用手搬運。整天就是抽啊、抽啊、抽啊。想像一下，每次打開水龍頭前都得先抽水！傑克和吉兒覺得拉長把手、看水從出水口流出來很好玩。水濺到地上時，他們咯咯笑著跳來跳去。他們玩得非常開心，但我敢肯定，如果他們必須像我媽媽一家人那樣每天做，這就不會那麼有趣了。

Unit 6: Skating on the Pond

⬇ PDF

This is my friend's son. His name is Brendan. Brendan is 3 years old. He has very blonde hair that is almost white. He always smiles and has very good manners. He is a good boy.

Brendan loves to go ice skating. It is his favorite thing to do in the winter. Sometimes he skates at the hockey arena, but usually he skates on a pond near his home.

Before he goes out, his mother always dresses him in winter clothes. They help to keep him warm even when it is cold outside. First, she puts long underwear and an undershirt on him. Then she puts on his pants and a warm sweater. Next she puts his ski pants and a winter jacket over the other clothes. Finally, she wraps a scarf around his neck, puts a toque on his head and pulls on his mittens. He is now ready to go.

The skates are put on when they get to the pond, as is the helmet. Brendan is too young to put his skates on by himself, so his father usually does it. He wears a helmet to protect his head incase he falls down.

Brendan can skate for hours without stopping. His mom always sits and watches him while she drinks hot coffee and talks to the other moms. She also brings a thermos of hot chocolate along for Brendan to drink when he takes a break.

When Brendan is finally cold and tired they head back home. After a day of skating, he always likes to eat a hot bowl of tomato soup to warm up. Then he often falls asleep on the couch watching cartoons. The fresh air and exercise is very good for a growing young boy.

課文朗讀
這是我朋友的兒子。他叫布蘭登，三歲。他有一頭幾乎像白色的金髮。他總是微笑，也很有禮貌，是個好孩子。布蘭登喜歡溜冰，這是他冬天最愛做的事。有時他在冰球場溜冰，但通常是在家附近的池塘上溜。在出門前，他媽媽總是幫他穿上冬天的衣服。即使外面很冷，這些衣服也能讓他保持溫暖。首先，她幫他穿上保暖內衣和內衣。接著穿上褲子和暖和的毛衣。然後再把滑雪褲和冬季外套穿在其他衣服外面。最後，她把圍巾圍在他的脖子上，幫他戴上毛帽，再套上連指手套。他現在準備好了。到池塘後才穿溜冰鞋，也才戴上安全帽。布蘭登太小，還不能自己穿溜冰鞋，所以通常由爸爸幫他。他戴安全帽是為了跌倒時保護頭部。布蘭登可以不停地溜好幾個小時。媽媽總是坐在旁邊看著他，一邊喝熱咖啡，一邊和其他媽媽聊天。她也會帶一瓶熱巧克力，讓布蘭登休息時喝。當布蘭登終於又冷又累時，他們就回家。溜了一天冰後，他總喜歡吃一碗熱番茄湯取暖。接著他常常在沙發上一邊看卡通一邊睡著。新鮮空氣和運動對成長中的小男孩非常有益。

Unit 7: The Balance Beam

⬇ PDF

This PE class is learning how to use the balance beam. A balance beam is nothing more than long square pole that runs horizontally above the ground. Young gymnasts who are just learning use a beam that is only a foot or two off the ground. Competitive gymnasts are required to use a beam that it much higher. Regardless of its height, it still takes a lot of practice to maintain your balance on the narrow apparatus.

In gymnastic competitions, such as the Olympics, gymnasts perform choreographed routines on the balance beam. Scores are calculated by several judges according to degree of difficulty, artistic merit and execution of the routine. This means that the more difficult and appealing the routine, it will receive higher scores than a simple routine as long as there are no mistakes. The judging system encourages gymnasts to push their limits and strive for perfection.

In the above picture, little Susan is practicing on the balance beam. She slowly places one foot in front of the other, keeping her arms spread out to help maintain her balance. If she falls, it is not far to the ground so she doesn't have to worry about getting hurt.

The other girls in the class are waiting patiently in line for their turn. For now, they are just practicing walking back and forth trying not to fall off. Once they have learned to this without falling off and have gained some confidence, their teacher will teach them increasingly more difficult moves. When they have mastered the fundamentals, they may be ready to learn a choreographed routine and take part in a small competition.

課文朗讀
這堂體育課正在學習如何使用平衡木。平衡木不過是一根長長的方形木桿，水平地架在地面上方。剛開始學習的年輕體操選手會使用離地只有一兩英尺高的平衡木。競賽體操選手則必須使用高得多的平衡木。無論高度如何，要在狹窄的器械上保持平衡，都需要大量練習。在奧運等體操比賽中，體操選手會在平衡木上表演編排好的動作。分數由幾位裁判依照難度、藝術表現和動作完成度來計算。這表示只要沒有失誤，越困難、越吸引人的動作會比簡單動作得到更高分。評分制度鼓勵體操選手挑戰極限，追求完美。在上方圖片中，小蘇珊正在平衡木上練習。她慢慢地一腳接一腳往前放，雙臂張開幫助保持平衡。如果她跌倒，離地面不遠，所以不用擔心受傷。班上其他女孩正耐心排隊等候輪到自己。現在她們只是練習來回走，努力不要掉下來。等她們學會不掉下來並建立信心後，老師會教更困難的動作。當她們掌握基本功後，也許就可以學習一套編排好的動作，參加小型比賽。

Unit 8: The Wedding Procession

⬇ PDF

When people get married, there is always a lot of decorating involved. The place where the wedding is held usually gets the most attention, but even the cars that the wedding party travels around in are adorned with colorful bows, ribbons and sometimes flowers. It isn’t hard to spot a wedding procession on the street; just look for the cars that look like they are wrapped up as gifts.

This particular car is for the bride and groom. It has a red bow on each of the door handles, long red ribbons running overtop the hood all the way to the trunk and a heart made from plastic roses on the grill. Other cars in the procession may or may not be decorated quite as extravagantly, but they will usually have at least some decorations to show that they are involved in the wedding.

In some areas of the world, it is customary for the wedding procession to drive through the streets honking the horn to garner attention. In smaller towns, people may run to the window or go outside to wave as the cars pass, but in larger centers they probably will only get an occasional glimpse if anything at all.

Chances are that one day you will ride in a car decorated this, whether you are getting married or maybe just a part of the wedding party. Maybe you will get to help decorate one of the cars, too. Who knows?

課文朗讀
人們結婚時，總會有很多裝飾。舉行婚禮的地方通常最受注意，但連婚禮隊伍搭乘的車子，也會用彩色蝴蝶結、緞帶，有時還有花來裝飾。在街上要認出婚禮車隊並不難；只要找那些看起來像禮物包裝一樣的車子就可以。這輛特別的車是給新郎和新娘坐的。每個車門把手上都有紅色蝴蝶結，長長的紅緞帶從引擎蓋一路延伸到後車廂，水箱護罩上還有一個用塑膠玫瑰做成的愛心。車隊中的其他車可能不會裝飾得這麼華麗，但通常至少會有一些裝飾，表示它們參與了婚禮。在世界某些地區，婚禮車隊開過街道時按喇叭吸引注意是一種習俗。在小城鎮，人們可能會跑到窗邊或到外面揮手；但在大城市，如果有注意到，也可能只是偶然瞥見而已。也許有一天你也會坐在這樣裝飾的車裡，不管是你自己結婚，或只是婚禮隊伍的一員。也許你還會幫忙裝飾其中一輛車。誰知道呢？

Unit 9: Valentine’s Day

⬇ PDF

Valentine's Day is a very special day for those of you who are romantic at heart. It's a time to show that special someone just how much you really care. It can also be a time to admit your feelings to someone for the first time.

Although some men may beg to differ, Valentine's Day is mostly for women and stores. It is the day when women want to be treated like queen or, at the very least, princesses. And remember that your queen or princess WILL be expecting something from you, so don’t forget to mark February 14th on your calendar…unless you enjoy the feeling guilty and being in the dog house. Phil is a very romantic young man. Today on his way home from school, he stopped by the local florist to pick up a bouquet of flowers for his sweetheart. Although his face doesn't show it, he cares deeply for Jill and he tells her this as he hands her the flowers. She blushes and accepts the token of his love.

Surprisingly, Jill actually has feelings for the stone-faced boy. She has even picked out a gift for him, too. It is a heart-shaped box of chocolates.

Phil is shocked that she cares about him. He can’t believe that the girl of his dreams likes him enough to buy him a gift. He is so overcome with joy that he begins to sob uncontrollably.

Jill is quite taken aback with his crying. Perhaps her feelings are not as strong as his overwhelming adoration of her. In a panic, she drops the flowers and rushes to the door.

Poor Phil can only watch as the door closes and his sweetheart exits his life forever. He drops to his knees holding his hands toward the skies. “WHY?!!” he blubbers. “WHY?!!”

Five minutes later Jill is on the next bus out of town.

課文朗讀
情人節對內心浪漫的人來說是非常特別的一天。這是向那個特別的人表達你有多在乎的時候，也可能是第一次向某人承認感情的時候。雖然有些男人可能不同意，但情人節大多是為女人和商店而存在的。這一天，女人希望被當成女王，至少也要像公主一樣被對待。請記得，你的女王或公主一定會期待你有所表示，所以別忘了在日曆上標記二月十四日，除非你喜歡內疚並惹她生氣。菲爾是一位非常浪漫的年輕人。今天放學回家途中，他到當地花店買了一束花給心上人。雖然他的臉看不出來，但他深深在乎吉兒，並在把花交給她時告訴她。她臉紅了，接受了這份愛的信物。令人意外的是，吉兒其實也喜歡這個面無表情的男孩。她也為他挑了一份禮物，是一盒心形巧克力。菲爾很震驚她竟然在乎他。他不敢相信夢中女孩竟然喜歡他到買禮物給他。他高興得無法控制，開始啜泣。吉兒被他的哭泣嚇了一跳。也許她的感情沒有他對她那種強烈愛慕那麼深。她慌張地丟下花，衝向門口。可憐的菲爾只能看著門關上，心上人永遠離開他的生命。他跪倒在地，雙手伸向天空。「為什麼？！」他哭喊著。「為什麼？！」五分鐘後，吉兒已經坐上下一班離開城鎮的公車。

Unit 10: Wedding Photos

⬇ PDF

It looks like somebody forgot to send out the wedding invitations! There isn't even a priest. And where are their parents? I'm no legal expert, but I think you at least need a justice of the peace and two witnesses to make a marriage legal.

Actually, the bride and groom are having their wedding photos taken. In Taiwan it is common to have professional wedding photos done prior to the wedding, sometimes weeks in advance. It takes some of the stress out of an already pressure packed wedding day when everyone is a little on the tense side. The time saved by having the photos done ahead of time can be better utilized mingling with guests and making the day more memorable rather than standing around waiting for someone to take your picture.

Another advantage of shooting the pictures in advance is that the photos can be developed and printed in time for the wedding. This is a big plus because the bride and groom can hand out the photos at the wedding instead of having to mail them out to everyone.

Scenic outdoor locations, such as this picturesque park, are often chosen for shoots. The natural beauty of areas like this provides a wonderful backdrop for pictures of any kind; though, in my opinion, they could lose the cheesy balloons.

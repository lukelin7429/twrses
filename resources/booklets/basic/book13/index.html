Unit 1: Parent-Teacher Association

⬇ PDF

The Sihu Senior High School Parent Teacher Association recently elected a new president. An inauguration ceremony was held to officially swear in the new president. The Chinese name for this ceremony is 交接典禮, which literally translated means passing-it-on. In other words, the old president passes the torch to the new one.

西湖高中家長教師會最近選出了新任會長。學校舉辦了一場就職典禮，正式宣誓新任會長就職。這個典禮的中文名稱是「交接典禮」，字面上的意思就是「傳承下去」。換句話說，就是前任會長將棒子交給新任會長。

Members of the PTA signed an attendance sheet at the door. This is a formality that takes place before all PTA gatherings so that there is documentation of who is present. The secretary of the group, who is most likely a volunteer, usually looks after these records.

家長教師會的成員在門口的簽到表上簽名。這是每次家長教師會聚會前都會進行的一項正式程序，用來記錄誰出席了活動。這個團體的秘書──很可能是一位志工──通常負責保管這些紀錄。

Once signed in, members were then asked to pin red ribbons to their shirts or jackets. Mr.Wu had difficulty attaching the pin to his lapel, so he asked Mary for assistance. She opened the lapel, carefully stuck the pin through it and then put the clasp on.

簽到之後，成員們接著被要求把紅色緞帶別在襯衫或外套上。吳先生在把別針別到西裝翻領上時遇到了困難，於是他請瑪麗幫忙。她打開翻領，小心地把別針穿過去，然後扣上扣環。

When the new president was formally sworn in, a handshake from the former president made it official. He was then presented with flowers and gifts from the PTA to congratulate him. To finish everything off, he gave a short speech to thank everyone for their support.

當新任會長正式宣誓就職後，前任會長與他握手，使這一切成為正式的事。接著，家長教師會贈送花束和禮物給他，以表達祝賀之意。最後，他發表了一段簡短的演說，感謝大家的支持。
課文朗讀

閱讀理解 Questions

What was the ceremony held for?

It was held to officially swear in the PTA president.

Why do members have to sign in?

They have to sign in so that there is documentation of who is present.

Why did the PTA give him flowers?

They gave him flowers to congratulate him.

Unit 2: Bending and Stretching

⬇ PDF

Look at the children stretching. They bend and stretch to make themselves more flexible. Stretching before any exercise is a good idea. It reduces the chances of injury.

看看這些正在伸展的孩子們。他們彎腰伸展身體，讓自己變得更柔軟。在做任何運動之前先伸展是個好主意，這能降低受傷的機會。

In dance class, flexibility is very important, so stretching is a must. The more flexible you are, the more dance moves you can do. The more dance moves you can do, the better a dancer you are. But it is also important to stretching exercises properly, or you could hurt yourself.

在舞蹈課上，柔軟度非常重要，所以伸展是必須的。你的身體越柔軟，就能做出越多的舞步。你能做的舞步越多，你就是越出色的舞者。但正確地做伸展運動也同樣重要，否則你可能會傷到自己。

First, their teacher shows them how to do each stretching exercise. Then the students try it. She watches to see that they do it right. If a student is doing something wrong, the teacher can help him or her out.

首先，老師會示範每個伸展動作該怎麼做。接著學生們會試著跟著做。她會在一旁觀察，確認他們做得正確。如果有學生做錯了，老師就能幫助他或她改正。

In the stretching exercise in the picture, the students lie on their stomachs and try to lean back as far as they can while bending their legs at the knees. Some of the girls are so flexible that they can touch the back of their heads with their toes. The teacher helps one boy to stretch way back. Hopefully she doesn’t break the boy in half!

在圖片中的這個伸展動作裡，學生們趴在地上，一邊彎曲膝蓋，一邊盡量向後仰。有些女孩柔軟度很好，甚至能用腳趾碰到自己的後腦勺。老師正在幫助一個男孩盡量向後伸展。希望她不會把這個男孩折成兩半！

Unit 3: Buying Tree Decorations

⬇ PDF

I get very excited every year when it is time to set up and decorate the Christmas tree. This year was even more special because Mom decided to throw out the old tree and buy a new one. We had been using that old tree for more than five years and it was starting to look a little worn-out.

我每年到了佈置聖誕樹的時候都會非常興奮。今年特別不一樣，因為媽媽決定把舊的聖誕樹丟掉，買一棵新的。我們用那棵舊聖誕樹已經用了五年多，它開始顯得有點破舊了。

My parents don't believe in using real trees. They think it is very wasteful to chop down a healthy tree that is just going to be used for a few days and then be thrown out. That is why we have an artificial one. If you ask me, they look just as good anyway.

我爸媽不相信要用真的樹。他們認為砍下一棵健康的樹，卻只用個幾天就丟掉，實在太浪費了。這就是為什麼我們用的是人造樹。要我說的話，人造樹看起來其實一樣漂亮。

The store we went to had lots of Christmas decorations. I swear that they had enough tinsel and garland to make Jay Chou jealous. There were hundreds of tree ornaments there, as well. Some of them were the pretty glass ones that I like, but Mom opted for the cheap plastic kind because my cat would probably just break anything that was too fragile anyway.

我們去的那家店有很多聖誕裝飾品。我敢說他們的金銀絲和彩帶多到能讓周杰倫都眼紅。店裡也有好幾百種聖誕樹吊飾。有些是我喜歡的漂亮玻璃吊飾，但媽媽選擇了便宜的塑膠吊飾，因為我的貓大概會把任何太脆弱的東西弄破。

Most of the ornaments came in packs of six or eight. I wanted to get blue and yellow ones, but my mom wanted green and red. Because we couldn’t agree on the colors for the tree, we picked up 4 variety packs.

大部分的吊飾都是六個或八個一組。我想買藍色和黃色的，但我媽想要綠色和紅色的。因為我們沒辦法在聖誕樹的顏色上達成共識，所以我們就買了四組綜合包。

We didn't have to buy new lights this year, but we still looked at them anyway. I wished that we would buy new ones so that I wouldn’t have to untangle the old strands like I do every year. And ours don't blink either, like some of the new fancier ones that make the tree look like it is sparkling. Oh well, maybe next year.

今年我們不需要買新的燈飾，但還是順便看了一下。我很希望我們能買新的，這樣我就不用像每年一樣去解開舊燈線的纏結了。而且我們家的燈也不會閃爍，不像有些比較炫的新款燈飾，能讓聖誕樹看起來閃閃發光。唉，也許明年吧。
課文朗讀

Unit 4: Opening Ceremony

⬇ PDF

Yesterday there was a track meet at my school. I didn't take part in any of the events or the opening ceremonies, but I still enjoyed watching everything. Even though I am not really into track and field, at least I didn’t have to go to class for the day.

昨天我們學校舉辦了一場田徑運動會。我雖然沒有參加任何項目或開幕典禮，但還是很享受在一旁觀看整個過程。雖然我對田徑其實沒有太大興趣，至少那天不用去上課。

The opening ceremony seemed quite official. It was almost like watching a real Olympic ceremony. A flag bearer led each class’s athletes onto the field. The procession marched once around the track, smiling and waving to the crowd.

開幕典禮看起來相當正式，簡直就像在看真正的奧運開幕式一樣。每個班級都由一名掌旗手帶領該班的運動員進場。隊伍繞跑道行進了一圈，一邊微笑一邊向群眾揮手。

After one loop around the track, each class lined up behind their respective leaders and flag bearers. It was all done in a very militant style. I was surprised to see that even some of my unruly classmates were capable of standing so still and being so quiet for more than ten seconds.

繞跑道走完一圈後，每個班級都在各自的班長和掌旗手後面排成一列。整個過程都是以一種相當軍事化的方式進行。我很驚訝地發現，就連班上一些平常很難管教的同學，居然也能站得那麼穩、安靜超過十秒鐘。

Once everyone was standing in place, six cheerleaders ushered out the school flag. They marched once around the track in their knee-high boots and short skirts. They must have done their job because the whole crowd cheered as they passed.

等大家都站定位後，六名啦啦隊員引領校旗出場。她們穿著及膝長靴和短裙繞跑道走了一圈。她們一定表現得很出色，因為她們經過時全場觀眾都歡呼了起來。

All in all, it was pretty impressive that a bunch of my schoolmates could put together such a wonderful opening ceremony. Maybe they are more organized than they seem. They certainly did a better job than I would have believed.

總而言之，我的同學們竟然能一起籌辦出這麼精彩的開幕典禮，實在令人印象深刻。也許他們比看起來還要有條理。他們的表現確實比我原本想像的要好得多。
課文朗讀

Unit 5: Pumps in the Park

⬇ PDF

Jack and Jill are playing with the pumps in the park. Not so long ago, everyone had to use pumps to get water out of the ground. This was before electric pumps.

傑克和吉兒正在公園裡玩抽水幫浦。不久以前，大家都得靠抽水幫浦才能把水從地底抽出來，那是在還沒有電動抽水機的年代。

I can remember filling the water trough for Grandma's cows by hand. It took a long time to fill the very large trough. It had to be done two times a day because the cows drank a lot. If it was really hot out, then it had to be done even more often because the cows would drink even more water. It was hard work, but it made me a strong boy.

我還記得曾經用手替奶奶的牛填滿水槽。要裝滿那個非常大的水槽得花上好長一段時間，而且一天得做兩次，因為牛喝很多水。如果天氣特別炎熱，就得做得更頻繁，因為牛會喝更多水。這是很辛苦的工作，但也讓我變成一個強壯的男孩。

When my mother was a girl, her and her family had to pump their own water for everything. If you wanted to take a drink you had to pump. If you wanted to wash your clothes, you had to pump. If you wanted to take a bath, you had to pump. Not to mention all the water they had to pump and haul by hand for the farm animals. Pump, pump, pump, all day long. Imagine having to pump water every time you turn on the tap!

This PE class is learning how to use the balance beam. A balance beam is nothing more than long square pole that runs horizontally above the ground. Phil is a very romantic young man. Today on his way home from school, he stopped by the local florist to pick up a bouquet of flowers for his sweetheart. Although his face doesn't show it, he cares deeply for Jill and he tells her this as he hands her the flowers. She blushes and accepts the token of his love.

雖然有些男士可能不敢苟同，但情人節主要是為女性和商家而存在的節日。這是女性希望被當作女王，或至少是公主般對待的日子。而且要記得，你的女王或公主一定會期待你送她禮物，所以千萬別忘了在行事曆上標記二月十四日……除非你喜歡那種內疚又被冷落的感覺。菲爾是個非常浪漫的年輕人。今天放學回家的路上，他順道去了當地的花店，為他的心上人買了一束花。雖然他的表情看不出來，但他其實非常在乎吉兒，他一邊把花遞給她，一邊告訴她這份心意。她臉紅了，接受了這份代表他愛意的禮物。

Surprisingly, Jill actually has feelings for the stone-faced boy. She has even picked out a gift for him, too. It is a heart-shaped box of chocolates.

令人驚訝的是，吉兒其實對這個面無表情的男孩也有感覺。她甚至還為他挑選了一份禮物——一盒心形的巧克力。

Phil is shocked that she cares about him. He can’t believe that the girl of his dreams likes him enough to buy him a gift. He is so overcome with joy that he begins to sob uncontrollably.

菲爾很驚訝她竟然在乎他。他不敢相信自己夢寐以求的女孩竟然喜歡他到願意送他禮物。他喜悅得難以自持，開始無法控制地啜泣起來。

Jill is quite taken aback with his crying. Perhaps her feelings are not as strong as his overwhelming adoration of her. In a panic, she drops the flowers and rushes to the door.

吉兒被他的哭泣嚇了一跳。也許她的感情並沒有他那樣強烈的愛慕之情。她驚慌之下，把花丟在地上，衝向了門口。

Poor Phil can only watch as the door closes and his sweetheart exits his life forever. He drops to his knees holding his hands toward the skies. “WHY?!!” he blubbers. “WHY?!!”

可憐的菲爾只能眼睜睜看著門關上，他的心上人永遠地離開了他的生命。他雙膝跪地，雙手舉向天空。「為什麼？！」他哭哭啼啼地喊著。「為什麼？！」

Five minutes later Jill is on the next bus out of town.

五分鐘後，吉兒已經坐上了離開小鎮的下一班公車。

He should never forget because his wife or girlfriend would be very, very angry.

We can’t tell how he feels because his face is emotionless.

Does Jill like Phil as much as he likes her?

No, he likes her a lot more.

Unit 10: Wedding Photos

⬇ PDF

It looks like somebody forgot to send out the wedding invitations! There isn't even a priest. And where are their parents? I'm no legal expert, but I think you at least need a justice of the peace and two witnesses to make a marriage legal.

看起來好像有人忘了發婚禮邀請函！現場甚至連神父都沒有。他們的父母又在哪裡呢？我不是法律專家，但我認為結婚至少需要一位治安法官和兩位見證人才能使婚姻合法。

Actually, the bride and groom are having their wedding photos taken. In Taiwan it is common to have professional wedding photos done prior to the wedding, sometimes weeks in advance. It takes some of the stress out of an already pressure packed wedding day when everyone is a little on the tense side. The time saved by having the photos done ahead of time can be better utilized mingling with guests and making the day more memorable rather than standing around waiting for someone to take your picture.

事實上，這對新人正在拍攝他們的婚紗照。在台灣，於婚禮前先拍好專業婚紗照是很常見的做法，有時甚至提前好幾週進行。這樣做能為原本壓力就很大的婚禮當天減輕一些壓力，畢竟那天每個人多少都會有點緊張。事先拍好照片所省下的時間，可以更妥善地用來和賓客交際往來、讓這一天更加難忘，而不是站在那裡等著別人幫你拍照。

Another advantage of shooting the pictures in advance is that the photos can be developed and printed in time for the wedding. This is a big plus because the bride and groom can hand out the photos at the wedding instead of having to mail them out to everyone.

提前拍攝照片的另一個好處是，照片可以及時沖洗、沖印出來，趕在婚禮當天使用。這是一大優點，因為新娘與新郎可以直接在婚禮上把照片發給大家，而不必事後再一一寄送給每個人。

Scenic outdoor locations, such as this picturesque park, are often chosen for shoots. The natural beauty of areas like this provides a wonderful backdrop for pictures of any kind; though, in my opinion, they could lose the cheesy balloons.

像這座風景如畫的公園一樣的優美戶外景點，經常被選作拍攝地點。這類地方的自然美景為各式各樣的照片提供了絕佳的背景；不過依我之見，那些俗氣的氣球還是拿掉比較好。

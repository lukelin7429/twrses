Unit 1: Andy’s Computer Problems

⬇ PDF
Andy

Darn it! My computer froze again.

Tony

Why does it keep doing that?

Andy

Someone sent a file to my email that had a virus attached to it and I opened it.

Tony

So your computer keeps freezing?

Andy

Yeah, but that’s not all. Windows pop up repeatedly, programs close without notice, and files seem to be disappearing.

Tony

Sounds like your computer has a mind of its own.

Andy

You can say that again. But what can I do?

Tony

I think you need to invest in some anti-virus software.

Andy

Man, that is such a scam. You know it is the people who sell the software that make the viruses, don’t you?

Tony

Maybe, but what choice do you have?

Andy

I’ll just reformat my hard drive and reinstall the operating system. That’ll fix it.

Tony

Wouldn’t it be easier just to buy the software?

Andy

Possibly, but this way is more reliable. After that I’ll just have to be more careful opening things that people send to me.

Tony

Suit yourself.

課文朗讀（真人）
安迪：真要命，我的電腦又當機了。 湯尼：為什麼它會一直當機呢？ 安迪：有人寄了一封夾帶病毒檔案的郵件到我的信箱中，而且我把它打開來看。 湯尼：所以你的電腦就一直當機了嗎？ 安迪：是啊！但不只是這樣，視窗不斷地彈出，程式在無預警下就關閉，而且檔案似乎一直消失。 湯尼：聽起來就像是你的電腦有自我意志一樣。 安迪：我完全同意，但我能做什麼呢？ 湯尼：我認為你需要花錢買些防毒軟體。 安迪：天啊！那真是一個騙局。你知道電腦病毒是賣防毒軟體的人作出來的，不是嗎？ 湯尼：或許是吧，但你還有什麼選擇嗎？ 安迪：我只要把我的硬碟重新格式化，並且重新安裝作業系統，那就能修好了。 湯尼：那購買防毒軟體豈不是更加容易嗎？ 安迪：也許是吧，但是這樣比較可靠。以後只需在開啟別人寄給我的檔案時小心一點就好了。 湯尼：隨你便吧！

閱讀理解 Questions

What keeps happening to Andy’s computer?

It keeps freezing up.

How did he get a virus?

He opened an email that had a virus attached to it.

What does Tony think Andy should do?

Tony thinks Andy should buy anti-virus software.

Unit 2: The Handy Backscratcher

⬇ PDF
Eric

Can you give me a hand with something?

Coco

Sure. (She begins clapping.)

Eric

Oh thanks, you are hilarious.

Coco

Okay. Okay. What do you want help with?

Eric

I have a really itchy back and I can’t reach it to scratch. Can you scratch it for me?

Coco

Yeah, right! I wouldn’t touch you with a ten-foot pole. But I do happen to have a backscratcher in my backpack. (She hands him the backscratcher.) Here you go.

Eric

(He grabs the backscratcher and starts scratching his back like a mad man.) Thanks. That feels much better.

Coco

You owe me one. Remember the old saying: You scratch my back and I’ll scratch yours.

Eric

Right. I won’t forget.

Coco

You’d better not forget, if you know what’s good for you. Questions: What is the backscratcher made from? Why is Eric looking for help? Does Coco want to scratch his itch for him? Why not?

課文朗讀（真人）
這是一支竹子做的長柄扒。它有一支細長扁平的柄，而且一邊的末端是彎曲的。彎曲的這一端被做成像手一樣的形狀，對於那些很癢卻很難觸及的部位，這種設計是很棒的，像是背部中間的癢處。 艾瑞克正在尋求幫忙，他背部很癢，但是他就是抓不到。他發現蔻蔻就坐在長椅上，他想知道她是否可以幫他的忙。 對話艾瑞克：妳可以用幫我做件事嗎？ (註：give sb. a hand一語雙關，它除了幫忙之外，還有拍手的意思) 蔻蔻：當然。（她開始拍手鼓掌） 艾瑞克：哦，謝謝，妳很好笑耶！ 蔻蔻：好啦，好啦，你想要我幫你什麼忙呢？ 艾瑞克：我背部很癢，但是我就是抓不到。妳可以幫我抓一下嗎？ 蔻蔻：是啊，沒錯！就算用十呎長的竿子，我也不會想要幫你抓癢。但是我的背包裡剛好有一支長柄扒。（她把長柄扒遞給他）給你。 艾瑞克：（他拿起長柄扒，像個瘋子似的開始抓他背部的癢處。）多謝。感覺好多了。 蔻蔻：你欠我一個人情。記得俗話說：你幫我的背部抓癢，我就會幫你的背部抓癢(註：人應當彼此互相幫忙)。 艾瑞克：是的，我不會忘記的。 蔻蔻：你最好不要忘記，如果你知道怎麼做對你才是好的。 Answers: It is made from bamboo. He is looking for help because he has an itch on his back that he can’t reach. No, she doesn’t because she doesn’t want to touch him.

Unit 3: Sharing

⬇ PDF
Grace

Umm! This chocolate bar is so good.

Julie

Can I have some?

Grace

No way! You never share with me when you have a treat.

Julie

That’s because you’re a pig and always take too much.

Grace

I am not a pig. You are!

Julie

(Starts to cry.) You are so mean. I am your little sister and you should take care of 5me.

Grace

Okay, okay. Stop your crying. Here you go. Grace hands her a piece of chocolate.

Julie

You are the best sister ever.

Grace

Remember that the next time I want some of your candy.

Julie

I will. Don’t worry.

葛芮絲(姊姊) 朱莉(妹妹) 葛芮絲：嗯！這個巧克力棒真好吃。 朱莉：可以給我一些嗎? 葛芮絲：想都別想！當你有好吃的東西時，你從來沒有和我分享過。 朱莉：那是因為你是隻豬，總是拿走好多。 葛芮絲：我不是豬，你才是! 朱莉：(開始哭泣) 你好吝嗇，我是你的妹妹，你應該要照顧我啊。 葛芮絲：好啦，好啦，你不要哭了，這些給你。 格雷斯給她一些巧克力。 朱莉：你是最好的姊姊。 葛芮絲：記得下次要給我一些你的糖果。 朱莉：我會給你的，別擔心。

閱讀理解 Questions

Why does Julie start to cry?

She starts to cry because her big sister is being mean to her.

Why does Julie say that Grace is the best sister ever?

Because Grace finally gives her a piece of the chocolate.

Unit 4: Moon Festival BBQ

⬇ PDF
Joe

What did you do for the Moon Festival?

Ted

I went to my parent’s house for a BBQ.

Joe

How was that?

Ted

It was great. My mother prepared a lot of different foods for us to eat.

Joe

Is your mother a good cook?

Ted

Yes, she is, but she only prepared the food. My father actually did the cooking on the BBQ as he always does. He thinks he is the best.

Joe

That’s funny! My dad is the same way. He thinks he is the only one that can cook on a BBQ.

Ted

Yeah. I think everyone’s dad thinks that they are the best BBQ-er.

Joe

True, but it is always the moms left to clean up the mess.

課文朗讀（真人）
喬：你中秋節時做了什麼?泰德：我去我父母的家裡烤肉。喬：烤得如何呢?泰德：很棒。我媽媽準備了很多不同的食物給我們吃。喬：你媽媽很會做菜嗎?泰德：是的，沒錯，但她只是準備食物，實際上負責烤肉的人是我爸爸，這一向如此。他認為他最會烤肉了。喬：那真是有趣，我爸爸也是一樣的，他認為他是唯一會烤肉的人。泰德：是啊。我認為每個人的爸爸都認為他們是最棒的烤肉高手。喬：沒錯！不過留下來收拾殘局的人總是媽媽。

閱讀理解 Questions

What did Ted do during the Moon Festival?

He went to his parent’s house for a BBQ.

Who did the cooking?

Ted’s father did the cooking.

What do both of their dads think?

They both think that they are the best at cooking on the BBQ.

Unit 5: Buying Bread

⬇ PDF
Jack

I love the smell of a bakery.

Irene

Me, too. Every time I come here, my mouth starts to water as soon as I walk through the door. So, what do you need to get?

Jack

I need to buy some bread so that I can make a sandwich for my lunch tomorrow. Where is it?

Irene

I think the bread is over by the pastries. They find the area where the bread is.

Jack

Wow. There are so many different kinds of bread. It makes it hard to choose.

Irene

What is this long one?

Jack

That is garlic bread. It has butter and garlic already inside. You heat it up in the oven and then slice it. It is usually served with steak, pasta or Caesar salad.

Irene

I think I’ve had it before, but I don’t really like garlic that much. What are these ones that look like a bull’s horns?

Jack

They are called croissants. They are very popular for breakfast. They are usually heated and then you can put some butter on them. Sometimes, people even slice them in half and make sandwiches. Tuna salad and crab salad croissants are both delicious and great light lunches.

Irene

Look here! You can even get hamburger and hot dog buns here. They are quite hard to find in Taiwan. Maybe I should pick up a dozen hot dog buns and we can have a wiener roast at the beach this weekend.

Jack

That sounds like a great idea. Jack takes a couple loaves of bread off the shelf.

Irene

Is that all you need?

Jack

Yep. One loaf of white bread and one whole-wheat should get me through the week.

Irene

Alright. We can pay and go home.

Jack

On second thought, maybe I should grab a half a dozen doughnuts…as long as we are here.

Irene

I knew you couldn’t resist buying something sweet.

Jack

You know me too well.

課文朗讀（真人）
傑克和他最好的朋友艾琳在鎮上另一頭的麵包店。傑克：我最喜歡麵包的味道了。艾琳：我也是。每次我來到這裡，一走進大門後，我的嘴巴就開始流口水。那麼，你要買什麼呢？傑克：我要買一些麵包，這樣我明天就可以做三明治當午餐。它在哪裡呢？艾琳：我想麵包在那邊，就在糕餅區的旁邊。他們找到了麵包區的位置。傑克：哇！有好多不同種類的麵包，讓我好難選擇。艾琳：這個長長的是什麼呢？傑克：這是香蒜麵包，它裡面有蒜頭和奶油。你可以把它放在烤箱加熱，然後再將它切片。它通常搭配牛排、義大利麵或凱薩沙拉一起吃。艾琳：我想我之前有吃過這個，但是我真的不是很喜歡蒜頭。那麼這些看起來像牛角的東西是什麼呢？傑克：它們叫做可頌，當早餐是很普遍的。通常我們會把可頌加熱，然後在上面放一些奶油。有時候，人們甚至將它們切半做三明治。鮪魚沙拉和蟹肉沙拉做的可頌都是很美味，而且是很棒的清淡午餐。艾琳：你看這裡，你甚至可以在這裡買到漢堡和熱狗麵包。在台灣他們相當很難買到。也許我可以買一打熱狗麵包，然後，這個週末我們可以在海邊烤燻肉香腸。傑克：這點子聽起來很棒！傑克從架子上拿了兩條土司艾琳：你就只買這些嗎？傑克：是的。一條白土司和一條全麥土司應該可以讓我度過一個禮拜。艾琳：好。我們可以付錢回家了。傑克：進一步考慮後，我想既然都來了，也許我應該買半打甜甜圈。艾琳：我知道你是無法抗拒甜點的。傑克：你太了解我了。

閱讀理解 Questions

Why did Jack need to go to the bakery?

He needed to buy some bread to make sandwiches for his lunch the next day.

Why was Mimi surprised to see hot dog and hamburger buns there?

She was surprised because they are quite hard to find in Taiwan.

Was Mimi surprised that Jack wanted to buy some doughnuts?

No, she wasn’t because she knew that Jack couldn’t resist buying something sweet.

Unit 6: Chatting about Facebook

⬇ PDF
Frieda

Do you know what tomorrow is?

Tony

Yes, it’s your birthday.

Frieda

Wow! You got it on your first guess. How did you know that?

Tony

I saw it on Facebook.

Frieda

Really?

Tony

Yes, it sent me an announcement to remind me of your birthday.

Frieda

I didn’t know it could do that?

Tony

It can do a lot of things.

Frieda

I know you can play on-line games there and search for friends, but what else can you do?

Tony

You can join discussion or interest groups.

Frieda

What kind of groups?

Tony

There is a group for almost everything imaginable. I even saw a group for people who like bacon.

Frieda

That seems kind of stupid. Are there any useful groups?

Tony

Yes, I just joined the Up with People group. Now I can stay connected with some new friends that I met this fall.

Frieda

I really like exploring new areas in Taiwan. Do you think there is a group for that?

Tony

Probably. You can search the groups using key words. So if you type in “explore Taiwan” or “travel Taiwan” you will more than likely get a lot of hits.

Frieda

Assuming I joined a group like this, what can I expect?

Tony

There will be a lot of forums on related topics. Maybe people would share their stories about traveling Taiwan. There might be some recommended places to see. Also, you can expect to see a lot of pictures.

Frieda

That sounds pretty amazing. Can I upload pictures of my own?

Tony

Sure, you can even upload short video clips.

Frieda

Are book clubs very popular?

Tony

Yeah, as are groups for political and social discussions. You can join a group just to show support for a cause too.

Frieda

I can’t wait to go now and get on Facebook. I want to see exactly what groups there are there.

Tony

Maybe I’ll come over and show you how to search for groups.

Frieda

Sounds good. Well, I better get to class now.

Tony

Ok. Talk to you after school.

課文朗讀（真人）
一個名叫芙芮達的年輕女生和她的朋友湯尼正在學校的走廊說話，現在是休息時間，他們在等下節課開始。 芙芮達：你知道明天是什麼日子嗎 ？ 湯尼：知道啊，是你的生日啊！ 芙芮達：哇！你第一次就猜中， 你怎麼知道的 ？ 湯尼：我在臉書上看到的。 芙芮達：真的嗎？ 湯尼：它寄給我一個通知，提醒我你的生日。 芙芮達：我不知道臉書有這項功能。 湯尼：在臉書可以做很多事。 芙芮達：我知道你可以玩線上遊戲和搜尋朋友， 但是還能做什麼呢？ 湯尼：你可以加入討論或有興趣的群組。 芙芮達：什麼種類的群組？ 湯尼：只要你想的到的都有， 我甚至看過喜歡培根的群組。 芙芮達：那好像有點蠢，有其他好的群組嗎？ 湯尼：有啊，我剛加入Up with People群組，現在我可以和今年秋天認識的新朋友保持聯繫。 芙芮達：我真的很喜歡探索台灣的新地區，你覺得有這樣的群組嗎？ 湯尼：有可能，你可以用關鍵字搜尋這樣的團體。如果你打「探索台灣」 或 「旅遊台灣」，你很有可能找到很多的相關資料的連結。 芙芮達：假設我加入像這樣的群組，我能預期什麼 ？ 湯尼：會有很多相關主題的論壇，也許人們會分享旅遊台灣的故事，可能也有一些別人推薦的地方可看，而且你可以預期看到很多照片。 芙芮達：這聽起來太棒了，我可以上傳自己的照片嗎？ 湯尼：當然，你甚至可以上傳影片。 芙芮達：讀書俱樂部很普遍嗎？ 湯尼：是啊，與政治和社會議題的討論一樣普遍，你也可以單純加入一個團體表達對某個理念的支持。 芙芮達：我等不及要趕快去上臉書了，我倒想看看到底那裡有什麼群組。 湯尼：也許我可以過去教你如何搜尋群組。 芙芮達： 很好。 嗯，我得去上課了。 湯尼：好，放學後再說。

Wow. It sure is beautiful here.

Yes, and it is nice and cool up here, too. George hears something.

Did you hear that?

Hear what?

It sounds like bird? They both look up at the tree, but they don’t see any birds there. Then the sound starts again. It’s a high-pitched buzzing noise.

George

Do you hear it now?

Yes, but it isn’t a bird.

George

What do you think it is?

Helen

It sounds like a cicada to me.

George

Don’t move! I think I saw a snake. Helen stops deadin her tracks.

Helen

Where did you see it?

George

Over there, by that rotting log. As they watch the spot where George supposedly saw the snake, a tiny creature crawls on top of the log to bask in the sunlight. Helen laughs.

Helen

That’s not a snake. It’s a grass lizard.

George

Well I only saw the end of its tail and it looked like a snake to me. They decide to sit down on a big rock next to the path and watch the little lizard bathe in the sun. All of a suddenthey hear a shriek and the cute little lizard disappears.

Helen

What in the heck was that? It happened so fast that I didn’t get a good look.

That was a Common Kestrel. It is a small falcon that likes to feed on all kinds of little creatures.

Really?

They even eat venomous snakes, if you can believe it.

Wait for me!!!

Hey Joe! What do you think old Louie is going to wear today?

Probably, the red blazer and yellow tie he wore yesterday.

Do you want to make a bet?

Joe

Ok. You’re on.

Dan

What do you want to wager?

Joe

How about, if I win, you have to do my homework for a week?

Dan

And what if I win? I don’t need YOU to do MY homework. I don’t want to fail my class!

Joe

Ok. How about if you win, I’ll buy you dinner at MOS Burger.

Dan

Make it two dinners and you’ve got yourself a deal. The two shake hands just as Mr. Johnson walks into the classroom. Joe jumps into the air cheering.

YES! Red blazer, yellow tie! I win. You can pay up by finishing last night’s homework.

Dan

Great.

上課前，兩個學生正坐在教室裡。他們正在談論他們的老師。 丹：嘿，喬！你猜老路易今天會穿什麼呢？ 喬：可能是他昨天穿的紅色西裝外套加黃色領帶。 丹：連續兩天穿同樣的衣服？嘿，不可能吧！ 喬：你想打賭嗎？ 丹：好呀！我賭綠色花呢西裝和佩斯利渦漩紋花呢的領帶。 喬：好，我賭了！ 丹：你想賭什麼？ 喬：如果我贏了，你就得幫我做一星期的回家功課，可以嗎？ 丹：假如我贏了呢？我不要你幫我做功課，我可不想被當掉！ 喬：好哇！如果你贏了，我將請你到摩斯漢堡吃一頓晚餐，可以嗎？ 丹：兩頓晚餐，我就願意賭。 當兩人握手成交時，傑森老師剛好進入教室。喬高興的跳起來歡呼！ 喬：我猜對了！紅色西裝外套加黃色領帶。我贏了！你可以幫我寫昨晚的作業來償還。 丹：很好！

Umm. Uh!

No problem. Anything to help a friend.

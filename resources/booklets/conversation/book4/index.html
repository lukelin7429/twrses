Unit 1: Tickets for the Big Show

⬇ PDF
Kenny

What are you up to this weekend?

你這個週末打算做什麼？
Thomas

I was thinking about going to the concert in Taichung.

我在想要不要去台中看演唱會。
Kenny

Who is playing?

誰要表演？
Thomas

Jolin is doing a show at the baseball park.

蔡依林要在棒球場開演唱會。
Kenny

Really? If she is coming, I would’ve thought it would be sold out by now.

真的假的？如果是她要來，我還以為票早就賣光了。
Thomas

It is, but I have a friend who has tickets and can’t go.

是賣光了，但我有個朋友有票，可是他不能去。
Kenny

How many tickets does he have?

他有幾張票？
Thomas

He told me 4, but 2 are already spoken for.

他跟我說有四張，但已經有兩張被人要走了。
Kenny

That leaves 2: 1 for you and 1 for me.

那就剩兩張了：一張給你，一張給我。
Thomas

Not so fast. I wanted to ask Colleen to come with me.

先別急。我原本想約柯琳跟我一起去。
Kenny

You wouldn’t deny me the chance to see Jolin live, would you?

你該不會要剝奪我現場看蔡依林的機會吧？
Thomas

I know you are a big fan, but Colleen is much better looking than you. Sorry.

我知道你是她的超級粉絲，但柯琳比你好看多了。抱歉囉。
Kenny

A big fan? I am her biggest fan. I have every album she has ever made and every DVD, too. It would crush me if I didn’t get to see her. You know I haven’t had the chance before, right? Thomas (sigh of pity): I know. I know. Ok, you can have the tickets and I’ll take Colleen to the movies instead. Kenny (tries to hug Thomas): Thank you. Thank you. Thank you. Thomas (defending against the hug): Alright! I got it. Just stop with the hugs. Jeez.

超級粉絲？我可是她的頭號粉絲。她出過的每張專輯我都有，每支DVD也都有。如果沒能看到她本人，我會很崩潰的。你知道我以前都沒機會看，對吧？湯瑪士（同情地嘆氣）：我知道，我知道。好啦，票給你，我改帶柯琳去看電影。肯尼（想要抱住湯瑪士）：謝謝你！謝謝你！謝謝你！湯瑪士（抵擋這個擁抱）：好了啦！知道了知道了，別再抱了，天哪。
Kenny

Ok. How much are the tickets?

好的。票多少錢？
Thomas

My friend said they are 6000… Kenny (reaches for his wallet): No problem.

我朋友說一張六千元……肯尼（伸手去拿皮夾）：小事一樁。
Thomas

Each! Kenny (stops taking his wallet out): On second thought, maybe I’ll just wait for the Live DVD. Thomas (shaking his head): Some fan you are.

是一張六千元喔！肯尼（停下拿皮夾的動作）：讓我再想想，也許我還是等現場DVD好了。湯瑪士（搖搖頭）：你這算哪門子粉絲啊。
課文朗讀（真人）

閱讀理解 Questions

How did Thomas get a hold of the tickets?

He had a friend who had tickets, but couldn’t go.

Why did Kenny want to see the show so badly?

He wanted to see the show badly because he is Jolin’s biggest fan.

What changed Kenny’s mind about going?

The price of the tickets changed his mind about going.

Unit 2: The Shark’s Plight

⬇ PDF
Teacher

Ok class. Today we’re going to see how much you know about sharks.

好了，各位同學。今天我們要來看看你們對鯊魚了解多少。
Students

Cool. That sounds fun.

酷，聽起來很有趣。
Teacher

First of all, what is the difference between a shark and all other fish?

首先，鯊魚和其他魚類有什麼不同？
Jack

I know! Sharks are bigger.

我知道！鯊魚比較大。
Teacher

Yes, some sharks are bigger, but some are smaller, too. Anyone else?

沒錯，有些鯊魚比較大，但有些也比較小。還有其他人知道嗎？
Frank

Sharks don’t have any bones.

鯊魚沒有骨頭。
Teacher

That is right, Frank. Sharks don’t have bones. Their skeletal structure is made entirely of cartilage just like your ear.

答對了，法蘭克。鯊魚沒有骨頭。牠們的骨骼結構完全是由軟骨組成的，就像你的耳朵一樣。
Thomas

And sharks are the only fish that kill people.

而且鯊魚是唯一會殺人的魚類。
Teacher

That’s not true. Some fish kill people because they have poisonous spines. Really, though, sharks aren’t as dangerous as most people believe. Let me ask you this: How many people are attacked by sharks worldwide every year?

那並不正確。有些魚類會因為有毒刺而殺人。其實，鯊魚並沒有大多數人想的那麼危險。我來問你們：全世界每年有多少人被鯊魚攻擊？
Jeffery

I think about 1, are attacked every year.

我想大概有1人每年被攻擊。
Teacher

Wow, that’s a lot and very specific, but it’s wrong. How about you, Sonia? What do you think?

哇，那數字很大又非常精確，但是答錯了。桑妮亞，你覺得呢？
Sonia

I think it is probably closer to 5000.

我覺得應該比較接近5000人。
Teacher

Actually, on average, between 50 and 70 people are attacked by sharks.

事實上，平均每年有50到70人被鯊魚攻擊。
Michelle

That’s not very many.

那真的不多。
Teacher

No, it isn’t. Why do you think Sonia and Jeffery guessed such a high number?

沒錯，確實不多。你們覺得桑妮亞和傑佛瑞為什麼會猜這麼高的數字？
Sam

Because people are very afraid of sharks and there are lots of documentaries on TV about shark attacks.

因為大家非常害怕鯊魚，而且電視上有很多關於鯊魚攻擊的紀錄片。
Teacher

That’s right. People have a strange relationship with sharks; they are afraid of them, but they love to watch TV shows about them. The reality is more people are killed by bees or struck by lightning every year, but movies like Jaws and other horror films have created a great fear of sharks in people. Of the people attacked, how many actually die, Tony?

答對了。人類和鯊魚有一種很奇怪的關係，他們害怕鯊魚，卻又喜歡看關於鯊魚的電視節目。事實上，每年被蜜蜂殺死或被閃電擊中的人還比較多，但像《大白鯊》這類的恐怖電影讓人們對鯊魚產生了極大的恐懼。東尼，在被攻擊的人當中，實際上有多少人死亡？
Tony

Probably around 12.

大概12人左右。
Teacher

Yes, about 8 to 12 people die every year from shark attacks. What country reports the most shark attacks every year?

沒錯，每年大約有8到12人死於鯊魚攻擊。哪個國家通報的鯊魚攻擊事件最多？
Michelle

Australia.

澳洲。
Teacher

Many people say that, but the USA is actually number 1. Most attacks are in Florida. Surprisingly, if you are attacked there, the chances of it being fatal are almost 0. This is because there is a shark “nursery” in the area, so most shark attacks in Florida involve inquisitive young sharks that aren’t big enough to cause much damage.

很多人都這麼說，但其實美國才是第一名。大多數攻擊事件發生在佛羅里達州。令人驚訝的是，如果你在那裡被攻擊，致命的機率幾乎是零。這是因為那個地區有一個鯊魚「育嬰場」，所以佛羅里達大多數的鯊魚攻擊都是好奇的幼鯊所為，牠們還不夠大，造成不了太大傷害。
Henry

What kind of sharks are the most dangerous?

哪種鯊魚最危險？
Alan

The Great White Shark.

大白鯊。
Teacher

Yes, that is one. What are the other two?

沒錯，那是其中一種。另外兩種是什麼？
Ann

The Whale Shark.

鯨鯊。
Teacher

That’s crazy! Whale sharks are one of the most passive creatures in the ocean. Plus, they don’t have teeth.

太扯了！鯨鯊是海裡最溫順的生物之一。而且，牠們根本沒有牙齒。
Tony

The tiger shark and the bull shark?

虎鯊和公牛鯊呢？
Teacher

Yes, they are the other 2. What makes the bull shark exceptionally different from most other sharks?

沒錯，那是另外兩種。是什麼讓公牛鯊和大多數其他鯊魚特別不同？
Alan

It can live for long periods of time in fresh water.

牠可以在淡水中生活很長一段時間。
Teacher

Yes, Alan. How did you know that?

沒錯，艾倫。你是怎麼知道的？
Alan

Learning about sharks is one of my hobbies.

了解鯊魚是我的嗜好之一。
Teacher

Good for you. That is a great hobby.

真棒。那是一個很棒的嗜好。
Alan

Did you know that bull sharks have been found hundreds of km’s upstream in many rivers?

你們知道嗎，人們曾在許多河流的上游好幾百公里處發現公牛鯊。
Teacher

I didn’t know that. Thanks, Alan.

我不知道耶。謝謝你，艾倫。
Bess

I heard that people kill many sharks every year just for their fins.

我聽說人們每年為了魚鰭而獵殺很多鯊魚。
Teacher

Yes, but how many? Do you know?

沒錯，但是有多少呢？你們知道嗎？
Sonia

5000?

5000隻？
Teacher

Not even close.

差得遠了。
Sam

5 million.

500萬隻。
Teacher

Yes, if you multiply that number by 20. The answer is over 100, 000, 000 sharks are slaughtered every year.

沒錯，如果你把那個數字乘以20。答案是每年有超過一億隻鯊魚被屠殺。
Tim

That’s terrible. What can we do to help the sharks?

太可怕了。我們能做些什麼來幫助鯊魚呢？
Teacher

Don’t buy shark meat, shark fin soup or any pills that contain shark byproducts.

不要買鯊魚肉、魚翅湯，或任何含有鯊魚副產品的藥丸。
Sonia

Why do people take shark pills?

為什麼人們要吃鯊魚藥丸？
Teacher

It’s because shark cartilage helps people with joint trouble. It is kind of like taking glucosamine.

那是因為鯊魚軟骨有助於治療關節問題的人。這有點像是在服用葡萄糖胺。
Alan

Yeah. My grandpa takes them for arthritis. I told him about the shark killings, but he doesn’t care.

是啊。我爺爺為了治療關節炎在吃這種藥。我跟他說過獵殺鯊魚的事，但他不在乎。
Teacher

Well…All we can do is to inform people and make them aware of the sharks’ plight. And pray!

嗯……我們能做的就是讓大家了解並意識到鯊魚的困境。並且為牠們祈禱！
課文朗讀（真人）

閱讀理解 Questions

What do sharks have instead of bones?

They have cartilage.

What is shark cartilage used for?

It is used to make pills for various joint problems.

What can you do about it?

I can stop buying things made from or of sharks and inform people of the shark’s plight.

Unit 3: Endangered Sea Turtles

⬇ PDF
Darcy

What are you reading about, Chuck?

查克，你在讀什麼？
Chuck

I’m reading about Green Sea Turtles. It’s a kind of sea turtle.

我在讀關於綠蠵龜的資料。牠是一種海龜。
Darcy

Oh. You mean like the Loggerhead Sea Turtle?

喔，你是說像蠵龜那樣的嗎？
Chuck

Yes. Do you like sea turtles?

對啊。你喜歡海龜嗎？
Darcy

I guess, but I don’t know much about them.

算是吧，但我對牠們了解不多。
Chuck

I can teach you about them.

我可以教你關於牠們的事。
Darcy

Ok. Tell me about them.

好啊，跟我說說牠們的事吧。
Chuck

Did you know that many sea turtles are endangered?

你知道嗎，很多海龜都是瀕臨絕種的。
Darcy

Actually, I did. I also heard that many different species used to lay their eggs on the beaches of Taiwan.

其實我知道。我也聽說過去有很多不同的物種曾在台灣的沙灘上產卵。
Chuck

Yes, but the green turtle is the only one that still does.

沒錯，但綠蠵龜是唯一至今仍在產卵的一種。
Darcy

Why is that?

為什麼會這樣？
Chuck

Well, before 1992 Taiwan didn’t do anything to protect their nesting areas and a lot of sites were lost because of urbanization.

嗯，在1992年以前，台灣沒有採取任何行動保護牠們的築巢地，因為都市化的關係，很多地點都消失了。
Darcy

Do they protect them now?

他們現在有保護嗎？
Chuck

Yes, but only few spots on the surrounding islands of Taiwan remain.

有，但台灣周圍的島嶼上只剩下少數幾個地點了。
Darcy

Is that the main reason their populations have declined.

那是牠們數量減少的主要原因嗎？
Chuck

Really, it is much more horrifying than that. People catch them for their meat, their shells (carapaces) are used to make decorations, and some people even try to keep them as pets.

說真的，情況比那個更可怕。人們捕捉牠們是為了肉，牠們的殼（甲殼）被拿來做裝飾品，有些人甚至想把牠們當寵物養。
Darcy

My grandpa told me that some temples buy them and put them on display.

我爺爺告訴我，有些寺廟會買下牠們並展示出來。
Chuck

That is strange considering that in Chinese culture they are a symbol of luck, prosperity and longevity.

這很奇怪，因為在中華文化裡牠們是幸運、繁榮和長壽的象徵。
Darcy

I know. You’d think people would want to protect them because of cultural importance.

我知道啊。你會以為人們會因為文化上的重要性而想保護牠們。
Chuck

I guess the truth is that many people here are just not informed about what is going on so the poaching continues.

我想事實是，這裡很多人根本不了解實際情況，所以盜獵才會持續發生。
Darcy

Are other countries the same way?

其他國家也是這樣嗎？
Chuck

I suppose a lot of countries are, but some are being very proactive like Costa Rica for example. My cousin went there for a wedding in February and he said that the turtle nesting grounds were fenced off. They even had guards patrolling the area to make sure that no one gets in.

我想很多國家都是，但有些國家非常積極主動，像哥斯大黎加就是一個例子。我表哥二月去那裡參加婚禮，他說海龜的築巢地都被圍起來了。他們甚至派警衛在那一帶巡邏，確保沒有人闖入。
Darcy

It’s good to know that some places are taking action.

很高興知道有些地方正在採取行動。
Chuck

It sure is and hopefully our country and others will start doing more as well.
